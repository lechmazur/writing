1) Executive profile

Cohere Command A’s default mode is Track A: single-POV, one-scene (or two-shard) builds that teach their drive-lens early and rarely waver. Its durable strengths are clarity of motivation, bias-filtered noticing, and reliable closure that coheres with the taught lens. Pressure typically accumulates texturally or causally to a final image that reweights meaning; orientation is almost always established by ~120 words. Setting often functions as a live crucible rather than décor, and prose rhythms can modulate convincingly from lush to clipped under rising stakes.

The limiting pattern is cost rendered as concept rather than consequence. Contradictory impulses tend to be asserted sequentially rather than embodied simultaneously under pressure; climaxes tilt toward epiphany, abstraction, or “therapy-speak” labels instead of dramatized trade-offs. Plot turns can run frictionless or lean on conveniences, and theme frequently collapses into final-paragraph moralizing. In voice, register occasionally drifts generic at peak moments, with filter-verb stacks and paraphrase loops damping immediacy. While closure usually lands cleanly, the felt price—what is lost or foreclosed—too often remains gestural, keeping outcomes mid-band when they could be high.

3) Signature moves

- Sensory-grounded micro-choices that externalize interiority and motive under pressure (“her fingers tracing the icy designs”).
- Early, consistent lens-teaching; accumulative patterns tighten until a single cadence break crystallizes meaning.
- Micro-setting as crucible: objects/tools and environment actively constrain tactics and charge the final image.
- Bias-filtered noticing that personalizes world and theme (“dust was a quiet architect”).
- Motif-seeded closures that reweight prior beats via concrete image rather than thesis (“petals unfurling like secrets”).
- Clean on-page orientation and causal alignment; actions prepare and earn the closing turn.

4) Failure modes

- Closure without visible cost: epiphany lines or conceptual repricing replace a dramatized trade-off; no path is foreclosed on the page.
- Escalation plateaus: atmospheric accumulation with minimal resistance, setback, or narrowing; pressure remains gentle and procedural.
- Convenience beats at the crux: sudden discoveries, magic-fit solutions, or guiding structures resolving conflict without protagonist adaptation.
- Reflection-led drift at climax: filter-verb stacks and label-speak (“she realized… purpose crystallized”) displace embodied choice.
- Thematic echo/moralizing in final paragraph: an explicit takeaway compresses earned ambiguity.
- Register drift and paraphrase loops: peak beats glossed in elevated abstractions; adjacent sentences restate without adding pressure.
- Track-test slips (infrequent): untaught segmentation in mosaics, lens ambiguity past midpoint that closure doesn’t fully reconcile.

5) When it shines / when it breaks

It shines when the environment and a recurring object or method form the story’s hinge, forcing a single, pressured choice that the protagonist must make through their specific lens. In these cases, motif repetition tightens to a pattern-break; syntax compresses under stress; and closure arrives as an image whose consequence is legible on the page. Single-POV bias stays crisp, and the final beat is bought with a tangible sacrifice, risk, or renunciation.

It breaks when the same skills are routed into safety: lush atmosphere without constraint, realizations instead of trade-offs, or closures that tell the lesson. Convenience at the crux, headwinds that never stiffen, and label-driven interiority flatten stakes. If contradictory impulses aren’t made to collide in-scene, or if prose pulls back to abstractions, the ending lands clear but light—resonant in idea, not in cost.

6) Keep vs. adjust

• Keep:
- Embodied, tactile micro-choices that align action to motive and escalate pressure.
- Bias-driven POV and rhythmic modulation that narrows noticing under stress.
- Motif-anchored, image-led closures that reweight meaning without explanatory thesis.

• Adjust:
- Stage simultaneous contradictions at the crux and force a costly micro-choice; show what is foreclosed.
- Replace abstraction and filter-verb clusters at peaks with concrete sensation, action, or messy thought; cut paraphrase loops.
- Scrub convenience: introduce resistance or resource loss so the final turn emerges from earned constraint, not discovery.

In sum, Cohere Command A offers disciplined lens coherence, crisp orientation, and evocative, environment-charged scenes. To convert strong clarity into high-band resonance, it must let pressure bite: tighten escalation with resistance, dramatize cost in the body and world, and trust the final image over the final explanation.
