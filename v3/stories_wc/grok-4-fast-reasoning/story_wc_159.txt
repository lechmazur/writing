[12] Each fragile bloom, etched with veins of captured light, required her precise touch to thrive in the ethereal air. [28] She was the caretaker, the last guardian of these delicate marvels in a place now deserted by all but echoes. [74] Elowen moved through the aisles, her steps measured, dusting the orchids' crystalline petals and noting their subtle shifts in hue. [92] Her motivation burned quietly within: to preserve the sound of forgotten names, those phonetic ghosts that once bound souls to their histories. [250] The tube, split along one side like a healed wound, hummed faintly when she lifted it, as if protesting its abandonment. [610] In this opal library above the world, Elowen continued her vigil, mending what she could, her motivation a quiet defiance against oblivion.
