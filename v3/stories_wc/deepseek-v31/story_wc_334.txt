[171] He knew, with the certainty of a folklorist, that it was a fairy ring. [186] And he knew the stories of what they could steal. [221] The air was cold and salt-bitten, the light thin and pale. [234]

[675] He had placed a single, glowing mushroom in Elias's small hand.
