[48] He was the man who painted souls, not smiles, because he could not see the latter. [87] He was, by necessity, very methodical. [93]

[240] But the woman on the canvas remained a collection of parts, a ghost without a spirit. [256] She was a technical exercise, and the drawing felt as cold as the case itself. [271]
