[100] His death by drowning was officially an accident, but his family insisted he was methodical, not reckless. [122] Elara was there to observe, to prove or disprove their claim through empirical observation. [142]

[165] Her headlamp cut a solitary beam through the oppressive gloom, its light skittering off pooling water and rusted pipes. [188] Then, her boot struck something metallic and hollow, its sound seeming to reverberate through the tunnel’s very bones. [212] She bent down, her gloved fingers brushing away silt and grime to reveal an old lantern, its glass panels caked in residue but its wrought-iron frame stubbornly intact. [303] She felt the desperate gasps of a fugitive, the fear a tangible taste on her tongue, the firm, guiding hand of a conductor, and the overwhelming hope that was both a shield and a torment. [346] These historical terrors and triumphs wove seamlessly with the more recent, sharper pangs of Leo’s own panic, his final moments of confusion and desperate yearning.
