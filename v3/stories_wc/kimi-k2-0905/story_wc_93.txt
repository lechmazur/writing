His nets lay empty beside him, for the eels had slipped away the moment the laws changed, their sinuous bodies no longer bound to the water's resistance. [65]
In his pocket, the pouch of collapsed star dust pulsed with a heat that defied the cold wind, a remnant from the night the sky had cracked open and rained fragments of dead suns upon the earth. [85]
