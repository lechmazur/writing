Her motivation was simple, irrational, sacred: to mirror the sky inside walls before the walls fell. [81]
Not metaphorically—literally. [83]

Her blindness to faces had trained her hands to see what eyes ignored. [214]

This was her method: not sight, but sensation; not observation, but embodiment. [254]
She began to craft. [257]
