[56] Her motivation was singular: to recover stolen memories, the lost weeks surrounding her mother’s disappearance decades later. [75]

[141] The event had coincided exactly with the blank space in her mind, a void that had haunted her for thirty years. [161]

[198] Elara paddled a small dinghy out to the site, the water so clear she could see the ghostly shapes of apple trees, their branches frozen in a silent dance beneath the surface. [228] She anchored directly over the dome, its curved glass slick with seaweed. [243] Taking a deep breath, she slipped over the side into the cold, silent world. [260]
