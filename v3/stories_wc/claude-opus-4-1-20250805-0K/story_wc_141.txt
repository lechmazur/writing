[19] Marina Voss, professional myth buster, climbed the rusted stairs with her equipment bag, humming unconsciously—a habit she'd developed after years of listening for lies in legends. [46]

[135] The scientific community had dismissed him as mad, but Marina suspected something more complex lurked beneath the ridicule. [154]
