[60] Tonight he will use it, not in wrath, but to shatter what the world thinks inevitable. [74] 

Because every gear he has ever built stomps predictably forward, and every pendulum obeys heartbeat regularity, Marrow is sick to the teeth of fate's metronome. [96] He wants drift, derailment, a sky that forgets its own dawn. [105] Therefore he came to this mysterious lighthouse on its rocky island, believing the heart of chaos hides in warped yesterlore. [123]
