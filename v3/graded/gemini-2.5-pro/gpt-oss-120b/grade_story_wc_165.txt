This robs the otherwise resonant actions of their subtextual power, telling the reader what to think instead of allowing them to feel it.</comment>
<question>6</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The narrative voice is distinct, with a lyrical and elevated tone that suits the analytical performer. <question>7</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The prose is ambitious and often beautiful, but it is hampered by a tendency toward overwriting.
