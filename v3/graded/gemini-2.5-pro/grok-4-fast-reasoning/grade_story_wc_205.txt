<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The stakes—cultural paralysis and societal drift—are clearly established and significant. However, the on-page conflict lacks pressure. <question>6</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>The narrative voice is exceptionally distinct, maintaining a consistent poetic and melancholic register that perfectly matches the protagonist and setting. <question>8</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The story's core conceit—a poet-revolutionary using visible timelines and myth-making to heal a societal schism on a baroque asteroid—is highly original and memorable.
