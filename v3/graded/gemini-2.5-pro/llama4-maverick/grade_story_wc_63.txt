The rake handle literally spelling out the theme is the most critical failure, replacing dramatic discovery with simple instruction.</comment>

This combination of repetition and overwriting makes the prose feel sluggish and didactic, particularly in the final third.</comment>
<question>8</question><grade>3.5</grade><comment>The story relies on familiar tropes—a tormented soul finding a cryptic message of hope, the clock as a metaphor for time and fate—without offering a fresh transformation.
