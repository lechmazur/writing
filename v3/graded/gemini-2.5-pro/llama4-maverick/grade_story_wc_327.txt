<question>4</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—preserving the essence of a dying language—is signaled clearly and provides a strong conceptual pressure. The resolution is earned within this framework, and the cost (the loss of the old language and the storyteller's voice) feels proportionate to the act of creation.</comment>
