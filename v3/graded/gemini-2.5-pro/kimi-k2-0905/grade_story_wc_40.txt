<question>4</question><grade>9.5</grade><comment>The story masterfully handles an abstract, internal stake: decoding meaning in a world that has stopped defining itself. <question>5</question><grade>9.2</grade><comment>Theme is developed with subtlety and power, emerging from the central conceit and culminating in a resonant final image. The score is slightly tempered by the penultimate sentence ("Growth was not always upward...") and the final sentence, which verge on stating the theme directly rather than letting the final image do all the work.</comment>

<question>7</question><grade>9.4</grade><comment>The line-level craft is superb, characterized by high information density, evocative imagery, and a confident, elegant rhythm. <question>8</question><grade>9.5</grade><comment>The premise of a “quiet apocalypse of language” following the last dictionary is highly original and thoughtfully explored.
