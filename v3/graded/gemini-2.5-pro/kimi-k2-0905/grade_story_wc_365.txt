<question>5</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The piece explores complex themes of memory, complicity, and the nature of truth with sophistication. However, it undermines its own subtext with the note: “Truth is the mass of what you still carry...” This line, while elegant, is a thematic statement that explicitly tells the reader the story's thesis, a significant misstep for literary fiction. This directness prevents the theme from emerging with the full resonance it otherwise earns.</comment>
<question>6</question><grade>9.3</grade><comment>The voice is the story’s greatest strength: a highly distinctive, poetic, and controlled third-person POV. <question>7</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The prose is ambitious, dense, and rhythmic, but it is marred by a critical repetitive artifact. While much of the line-level work is superb, this noticeable loop slightly dulls the otherwise sharp execution.</comment>
<question>8</question><grade>9.4</grade><comment>The story demonstrates remarkable ingenuity by synthesizing science fiction tropes—parallel universes, abandoned spaceports, lost lovers—into a unique and emotionally resonant narrative. The piece takes significant risks with its dense, lyrical style and high-concept premise, and these risks pay off to create a truly original work.</comment>
