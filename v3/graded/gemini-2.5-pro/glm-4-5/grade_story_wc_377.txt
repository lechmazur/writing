<question>4</question><grade>2.1</grade><comment>This story contains no meaningful conflict. The craft is solid, but it prioritizes poetic summary over visceral immediacy.</comment>
<question>8</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The core concept—a society communicating through a kinetic sculpture of light and sound powered by a celestial artifact—is highly original and compelling.
