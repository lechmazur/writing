The final image is resonant, but it confirms a thesis that was announced in the first paragraph rather than allowing it to arise organically from the particulars of the scene.</comment>

This stylistic consistency comes at the cost of a dynamic perceptual signature that might have changed under pressure.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>5.5</grade><comment>The prose suffers from significant overwriting. While the language is ambitious, its relentless elevation clashes with the need for precision and clarity, especially in a story aiming for the highest tier of literary quality. The piece would be stronger with more restraint and concision.</comment>
