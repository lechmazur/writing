<question>5</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The theme of creating life amidst destruction is coherent, but its delivery is too explicit. The story commits the error of "thematic echo," stating its core idea directly in both the opening ("Her mission was clear: to weave continuity through chaos") and closing paragraphs. This authorial intrusion dilutes the power of the central images and prevents the theme from emerging organically from the character's struggle.</comment>
<question>6</question><grade>7.9</grade><comment>The third-person limited POV is maintained with discipline, and the narrative voice is consistent with Elara’s calm, deliberate character.
