[655] The early future had rushed in because humanity had become ready for this merging sooner than expected. [685] As they emerged from the maze, now restored to full size in the cobalt glade, the visionary felt the burden of their root-bound existence lift. [721] They were still connected to the earth, yet free to expand their vision, now understanding that dreams and reality were not opposing forces but complementary ones.
