[253]

As she walked, memory after memory rose to the surface of her mind: a child laughing on a long-destroyed planet, a scientist sacrificing themselves to save their colony, a poet writing lines that outlived their entire star system, each of these moments sliding into the blank pages of the dictionary as she walked.
