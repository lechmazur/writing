Mara’s motivation was singular: to understand the architecture of lies that had scaffolded her father’s heroic reputation and, in turn, her own restless life. [90]
The pocket sundial had been his; its gnomon still bore the tooth-marks of a shell fragment he claimed never touched him, yet the dent told another story. [113]
