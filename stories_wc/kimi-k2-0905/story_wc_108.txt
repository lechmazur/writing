I stepped inside, my knees already knocking—Leo Vance, phobia-ridden stunt double, the man who flinched at his own shadow yet leapt off buildings for a living. [39]

Legend said it still murmured the last honest thing she ever spoke before the tyrant edited her out of the story. [107]
