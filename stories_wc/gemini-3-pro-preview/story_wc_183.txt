"We are walking past the concessions," she whispered firmly to the swirling gray air, weaving a story that forced the shifting steel to solidify into a recognizable shape. [267]
She sought to recapture the specific essence of the sister she had never met, hoping to separate the ghost from her own biology for just one moment of conversation. [296]

It was an artifact from a nursery that was never built, a sample of the world her sister would have known if she had survived to see the light. [348]
The intense green of the card acted as a beacon in the monochrome gloom, cutting through the fog that tried to erase her destination. [372]
As Elara moved deeper into the ship, the atmosphere shifted from eerie to blissfully aberrant, as if the anomaly of her own existence was finally at home among these broken rides. [403]
