<story>In a forgotten orchard deep in a bamboo forest, a wistful loom spinner listened to the groves breathe and measured possibilities by the tremor of leaves. [26]

Kio bought them with two apricots and a promise, because edges define a world, and she needed a border to keep herself from unraveling. [123]
