[20] In that particular quiet - the one that follows only after sound has fully spent itself - Sena lifted the storm glass vial from her coat pocket and read its crystals. [77] The terrace was named for the way its glass panels caught afternoon light and scattered it across the gondola's interior in slow, shifting lozenges of color - as though the entire platform breathed and wandered even while anchored to its cables. [362] Sena had come to love it for exactly that reason. [372]
