[79] Now it fit the curve of her palm like it had always belonged there, its surface worn thin in that one spot where she pressed hardest when the memories grew teeth. [107]

[176] The girl had been brilliant, fierce, difficult in the way that diamonds are difficult before cutting reveals their fire. [243] The covenant had been designed to protect small corruptions, minor cruelties, the usual rot of powerful families.
