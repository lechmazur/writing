[133] And somewhere in this spectrum, she believed, existed the precise pitch of trust as it fractured. [149]

[174] He couldn't hear normally anymore—every voice carried that phantom breaking. [185] Mira had promised him a solution, but first she needed to capture that specific resonance. [201]
