[27] She was a watchful dreamer, tending to the garden she'd coaxed from concrete, observing each visitor who found their way through the rusted doors seeking what she offered: a place to graft new meaning onto old wounds. [66]

[94] The video had spread like wildfire, igniting rage and, unexpectedly, a hunger for healing spaces where the harmed could gather without judgment. [115] Mira had quietly, proactively transformed this tomb of technology into something living, something that breathed forgiveness into the air. [135]

Today, she noticed the icicles along the building's north edge had begun their seasonal thaw. [150] Water dripped through gaps in the ceiling, leaving dark patterns on the moss below—irregular constellations that reminded her of the data maps she'd once seen, back when algorithms had tracked her every movement, every relationship, every whispered secret. [190] But these patterns were organic, unpredictable, honest. [197]
