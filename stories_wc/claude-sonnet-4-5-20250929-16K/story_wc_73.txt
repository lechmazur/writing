[50] She was a mathematician before this place claimed her, and she would remain one until she solved its impossible riddle. [71]

[109] Perfect randomness had died with that chip, but the flaw gave her something to measure against the staircase's own probability distortions. [131] Here, in this vertical infinity, chance bent in ways that suggested the unthinkable: true perpetual motion. [148]
