[274]The dictionary whispered definitions as I walked the circular aisles, and each definition changed depending on what I feared and what I hoped. [297]To keep from drowning in myself, I practiced being wanderingly exact, letting my mind roam through memories while my attention measured every footfall and every breath. [555]Up above, the temple library creaked as if waking, and I climbed back through the well with the softcover dictionary pressed to my chest, hearing in the silence the faintest chorus of unsaid questions.
