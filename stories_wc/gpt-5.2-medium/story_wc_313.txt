<story>After the wind chime on my porch fell silent, I knew the lake had dropped again. [62] My job is to keep the levee true, to warn tourists off weak banks, and to expunge any rumor that the dam is failing. [491] Now the reservoir town resurfacing was not a haunting but an audit, and I could either keep expunging or make room for truth. [514] I photographed the boot print, the flood marks, the bar ledger swollen shut, and the silent wind chime on my porch, turning these into a new trail for others.
