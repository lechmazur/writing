<story>Marta Vellum wrote a gossip column on weekdays and performed exorcisms on Sundays, and the two jobs paid about equally. [249] The perfume bottle was her vessel, inherited, cracked once and glued with egg white and patience. [265] It had belonged to her mother. [271] That was not an accident.
