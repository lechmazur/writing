<story>Bern had lived between the library's walls for eleven winters. [10] In the pipe chases above the reading room, he kept a cache of things the world had dropped. [28] Pencils. [29] Hair pins. [57] At dawn, when the shadowed library held its sapphire hush, he would slide along a duct and leave the objects on tables where the right person would find them. [86] No one knew him. [90] That was the arrangement he had made with himself long ago, and it had held until the night the city's lights flickered after an unexpected outage.
