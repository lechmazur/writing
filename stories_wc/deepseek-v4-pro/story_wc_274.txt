Korr's gloved fingers cracked the seal under the blue glow of his helmet lamp. [107]

He held it close and read. [128]

The contraband truths scorched his mind: the war that ended the world had been a lovers' stagecraft. [177]

To bury it again would make him complicit in the glacier of falsehood. [295]
