[22] The endless night outside refused even a sliver of moon, yet the greenhouse’s interior glowed with a sickly amber luminescence born of rotting alchemical fruit. [254] The paper felt cool as skin, and a single brass snap fastener kept its pages sealed. [270] The snap was shaped like a tiny cage. [278] Branoc knew what to do.
