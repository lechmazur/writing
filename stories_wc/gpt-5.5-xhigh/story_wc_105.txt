<story>In the sunken orchard greenhouse ringed by shipwrecks, Iren Vale built hearing aids from wreck brass, applewood, and the stubborn bones of radios. [23]

That strange hearing made him the best designer left along the northern shoals, because he could tune sorrow until it became bearable. [121]
