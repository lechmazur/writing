Mira Vale had been a wreck-diver ghost whisperer long before the living learned to fear her quiet mouth. [18]
She worked where the fjord narrowed under blue cliffs, in a candle-lit shrine carved into glacier ice, whose walls held every flame like borrowed starlight. [43]

The girl appeared in seventeen minds, always near a window, always before fire, never after water. [356]
