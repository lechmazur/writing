She had been cataloging sleep masks on page one, yet now, somewhere between the first and last page, she stood in the orchard that the book declared impossible. [60]

Under provenance, the label read nonlinear inheritance, and under restrictions, Do not inquire unless willing to become the ancestor of your answer. [144]
That warning should have satisfied a cautious scholar, yet Mara had joined the Reliquary to pursue forbidden knowledge, not to dust it for safer minds. [169]

You are late, said her mother, though grief made the sentence sound simply complicated, as if lateness were another name for love. [336]
