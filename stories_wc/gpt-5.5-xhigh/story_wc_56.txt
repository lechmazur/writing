Elian's father, the lock architect, had signed that report, then walked into exile without farewell. [139]

In full sun it was useless, but at dusk its gnomon cast a backward shadow across tiny canal marks no maker should have known. [191]

"You want treasure," she said. [220]

"I want hindsight clarity," he answered, surprised by how thoughtfully clear his voice sounded on the water. [257]
Tamsin spat overboard. [260]
"That costs more than gold," she said. [267]
