The ribbon, a bright crimson strip frayed at the edges, was the one her girl had worn on the day she first asked to learn the trade. [117]
Mira's heart clenched, for the hair ribbon carried a promise she had whispered to the child: that she would keep it safe until the leaves fell each autumn. [145]
