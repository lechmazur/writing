[42] Now, instead of a healed grove, a swirling orchard vortex churns at the heart of the cursed lake, pulling ancient trees into its gyre like splinters. [147] She steps onto the water’s surface, a simple druidic grace, and walks toward the maelstrom of wood and water. [260] And there, suspended in the eye of the storm, is her failure given form: a confession, frozen solid.
