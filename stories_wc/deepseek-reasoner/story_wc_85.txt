<story>Elara’s world was built upon the cobalt terrace at dusk, a suspended platform of deep blue stone that overlooked a whispering, grey sea. [62] They came to her black market operation to purchase new pasts, to fill the hollow spaces in their histories. [84] Elara manufactures false memories, delicate constructions of time and sensation woven from suggestion and desire. [104] Her own guilt was the loom upon which she built these beautiful lies for others; she had once altered a lineage for a client, an act that had unforeseen, tragic consequences for a true heir she never knew existed. [166] He believed his grandfather had been a hero, not the coward family lore suggested, and he needed a memory to prove it.
