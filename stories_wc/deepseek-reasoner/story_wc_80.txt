[86] Its lacquered roof, a pagoda of another era, breached the surface like the back of a sleeping leviathan. [130] The air held a hushed blaze of orange from the setting sun, a quiet fire that burned without sound. [147]

[202] It was not for drafting lines on vellum, but for a different, more sacred purpose. [218]
