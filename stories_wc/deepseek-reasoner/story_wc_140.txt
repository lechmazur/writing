[107] Their sole motivation was not to reclaim a name, but to discover the texture of memories that were not their own, to feel the weight of a past. [134]

[284] Its skull-and-crossbones was faded, but the word “ARSENIC” remained starkly visible, a dark and final pronouncement. [344] K filtered the entire labyrinth through its terrifying potential, their perception shifting to see hidden threats and whispered regrets in every shadow. [371] They returned to their collected fragments, cross-referencing tales of sudden illness, of hurried departures, of a wealthy family’s abrupt exit from the city years prior.
