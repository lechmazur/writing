[49] She was modestly fearless, not from a lack of feeling, but from a profound understanding that fear was just another frequency, one she had learned to tune out. [109]

[158] Elara knew standard methods would fail; this required a key to a long-locked door. [176] That key, according to ancient texts, was found through the distorted notes of a misaligned harp.
