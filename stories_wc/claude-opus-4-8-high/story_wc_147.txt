[476] Her lungs ached now, the air nearly gone, and the temple's hush pressed close like a benediction. [493] Mira kicked upward, the vial clutched to her heart, ascending through shafts of dim and trembling green. [510] Above her, faint and stubborn, the distress beacon she had sent still flashed across the dark water. [527] She did not know if any ship had answered it, or if she climbed toward open sky or only deeper night. [548] But for the first time in years she was not afraid, because she carried something the sea could never take. [568] If she surfaced, she would open the vial above her daughter's empty bed and let the laughter ring once more.
