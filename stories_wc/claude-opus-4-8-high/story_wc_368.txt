<story>After receiving the donor heart, Mira climbed to the floating rock tea house to learn why some people magnetize stories. [42] Inside, a watchful dye master named Oren tended vats that breathed indigo steam. [267] "The brightest things survive by showing their light only at the right angle." [440] It struck the blue morpho wing, and the whole room flared, walls swimming with shifting light. [510] "It was in the angle of attention, in someone willing to wait for the light." [552] She took the wing when he offered it, cupping its iridescent caution in her palm.
