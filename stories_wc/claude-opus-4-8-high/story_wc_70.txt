<story>After the city-wide blackout, Maro climbed the lightning collection tower with a candor he could not switch off. [233] A young engineer climbed up behind him, breathless, demanding to know the truth about the failure. [537] Routine was not the enemy of wonder; forgetting to look was. [548] 'Will it fail again?' Sela asked, and Maro answered with his unavoidable honesty.
