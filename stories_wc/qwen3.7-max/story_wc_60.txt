[21] She was known among the remaining archivists as a luminous skeptic, one whose questioning mind burned brighter than the dogma she challenged. [135] The box had been sealed for three generations, and Elara meant to open it tonight. [150] The geese had paused in their migration overhead, hanging in the amber sky like scattered notes of an unfinished song.
