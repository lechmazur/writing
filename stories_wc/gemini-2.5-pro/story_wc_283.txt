[110] His motivation was not to obey the prophecy, but to spin its rigid certainty into the raw possibility of a new beginning. [133] He believed the Founders’ prediction was not a curse but a warning, a knot of tangled history he had to unscramble. [155] His method was painstaking, almost monastic: by cataloging lost buttons.
