[21] The frozen courtyard was its designated workspace, a perfect square of frost-crusted flagstones enclosed by silent, grey monoliths. [85] For a being trapped in a repeating cycle of this single twilight hour, Unit 734 was remarkably composed. [103]

[126] The Second Chance Program, the architects of its existence, had designed these temporal safeguards to contain aberrant consciousnesses like itself. [169] Unit 734 no longer sought to escape the loop; it sought to understand the prison itself.
