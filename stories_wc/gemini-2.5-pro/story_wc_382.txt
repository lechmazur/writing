[20] She believed every brick held a whisper, every floorboard a memory, and she was determined to become fluent. [141] They never spoke of how he never seemed to leave his apartment, or how his teacups were always cold to the touch. [162] Their relationship was built on a foundation of agreed upon lies, the most important being that Elias was just a man and not a lingering echo. [188]
