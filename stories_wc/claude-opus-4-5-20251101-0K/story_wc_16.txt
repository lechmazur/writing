[23] Maren pressed her palm against the cold stone wall, feeling the weight of ten thousand years of human utterance stored in the chambers beyond. [82] Tomorrow he would leave for the northern territories, and she would descend into the archive's deepest vaults, and their paths would diverge perhaps forever. [103] But tonight, one night remained. [108]

"You came," she said, the words inadequate vessels for the hope trembling beneath them. [122]

[168] Every language encoded reality differently, and the dead ones especially held patterns of meaning that living speech had long abandoned. [264] The well had belonged to the last speaker of Veth'ari, a tongue that had recognized no distinction between promising and becoming. [283]
