[159] Each die’s tumble, each random conjunction, generated a new cypher layer, a lock within a lock protecting the Archive's core. [183] Elara’s purpose was to solve them all, as a spider spins its web, with patient, inexorable purpose. [205]

[288] She explains through dance the elegant, terrible pressure of the abyss, translating mathematical certainty into a sublime, physical language. [315] The recording was another line of code in itself, a beautiful and impenetrable record. [334]

[385] This vulnerability was a threat, a crack that could shatter her defiant calm. [402] So she did the only thing she knew: she began to encrypt it.
