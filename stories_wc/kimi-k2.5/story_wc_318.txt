[48] Now he existed as a mournfully eager phantom, clutching the translucent pull cord handle that had once summoned thunder from engines, desperate to restore the lost laughter to a household that had forgotten how to breathe without pain. [41] Elias pulled the cord again, mournfully eager, watching as the pull cord handle glowed brighter with each maceration of grief.
