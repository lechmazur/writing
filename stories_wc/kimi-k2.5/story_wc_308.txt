[37] He had not always been still. [121] The pulse left wandering—that erratic rhythm that had detached from its original heart and drifted through time seeking reunion—vibrated faintly within the artifact, a displaced beat that matched the skimmer's own suspended animation. [154] He needed to appraise its significance, to determine whether this fragment of violence held the key to his liberation or merely another anchor binding him to the dust. [289] The historically reverent nature of his meditation demanded he acknowledge every death that had fed these bone towers, every predator and prey whose calcium had built this labyrinth of endless echoes where sound never died but merely accumulated, layering upon itself until the air itself became a solid record of suffering and survival.
