[27] They had always known fear, every height, every fire, every fall on set sending their heart racing, but tonight the terror was different, intimate, a betrayal that rewrote their entire existence. [107] Their father, a legendary stunt coordinator, had raised them to replace his lost son, training their body while erasing their mind's fragility, never understanding that the phobias were not weaknesses but Kai's firmly adaptive survival mechanisms, hyper-awareness honed to a razor's edge. [243] Decrypting the message required Kai to embrace every panic response their father had tried to train away.
