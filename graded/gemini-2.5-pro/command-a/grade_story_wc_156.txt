<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The central conflict—methodical certainty versus surrendered curiosity—is clearly established and tied to the dominant stake of restoring the mural. However, the dialogue from the magical figures is stilted and serves primarily as thematic exposition rather than natural speech. <question>7</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The prose is generally clear and competent, but it relies on several clichés at peak moments, such as "heart raced," a "whirlwind of confusion," and colors "exploding into life." <question>8</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The core premise of a sound wave garden, a shifting mural, and a talking hobo nickel is wonderfully fresh and imaginative.
