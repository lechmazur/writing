<question>4</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—preserving wonder against a tide of communal apathy—is established early and made concrete through the magical ritual. The resolution is earned within the story's magical logic, and the cost is legible in the artisans' exhaustion and their lifelong commitment, which effectively forecloses a life of ease.</comment>

The prose has a clear perceptual signature and rhythmic control, effectively supporting the story's mythic quality.</comment>

While the craft is high, a more varied sentence structure with moments of plainer language would have allowed the key images to resonate more powerfully.</comment>

The story commits fully to its unique premise, creating a distinct and resonant vision.</comment>
