<question>4</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—preserving individuality and art against a homogenizing world—is signaled early and effectively deepened. <question>5</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The theme of forging beauty from brokenness is powerful and well-integrated into the story's imagery. This directness prevents the theme from emerging with the full, unstated power the preceding scene had earned.</comment>

The prose rhythm is controlled and modulates effectively, creating a narrative voice that is both memorable and perfectly suited to the story's elegiac, resistant tone.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>The line-level craft is strong, with evocative and concise prose that carries high information density. The only minor weakness is a slight tendency toward abstract summary ("a small revolution against the permanence everyone else seemed to crave") where a concrete image might have been stronger.</comment>
