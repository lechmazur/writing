<question>4</question><grade>9.5</grade><comment>The dominant stake—the survival of cultural identity, not just physical life—is established early and tested throughout. The resolution, a declaration of hope, is earned by the visible cost of their long endurance.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>9.4</grade><comment>The theme of survival as an act of cultural inheritance is seamlessly integrated. The story’s final image offers a resonant, non-literal answer to the central question of endurance, reframing it as a deliberate act of creation.</comment>
