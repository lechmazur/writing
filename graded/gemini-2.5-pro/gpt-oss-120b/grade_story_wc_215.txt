<question>5</question><grade>9.4</grade><comment>Theme is the story's core strength, emerging organically from a potent constellation of images: the silent ghosts, the sealed box, and the memory of bees. It masterfully avoids didacticism in favor of complex, lingering feeling.</comment>

The narrative voice is controlled and purposeful, contributing significantly to the story's atmosphere.</comment>
