The lack of sentence variety and a tendency towards summary over scene work creates a sluggish, declarative momentum.</comment>
<question>8</question><grade>5.1</grade><comment>The concept of using community gardens for coded messages is a fresh angle on the familiar dystopian trope of thought-policing.
