<question>4</question><grade>9.4</grade><comment>The conflict operates on a sophisticated, internal level, shifting the stake from solving a crime to finding a more humane truth. <question>5</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The theme of empathy as an act of "unifying opposites" is developed with extraordinary subtlety and depth through the story's central action and imagery. It functions as an explicit thematic statement that slightly undermines the profound resonance earned by the preceding scene, reducing a complex emotional resolution to a label. This unnecessary summary is the primary flaw in an otherwise thematically brilliant piece.</comment>

This is a rare example of a voice that is not just a style, but the story's entire mode of being.</comment>
