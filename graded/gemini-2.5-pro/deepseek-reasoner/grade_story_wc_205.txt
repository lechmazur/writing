The coincidence of finding the shard serves as a catalyst for this internal work, not a simple solution, which is a sophisticated structural choice.</comment>

Crucially, the setting constrains choice (covering the mirrors during the storm) and the key object—the shard—becomes a micro-setting in itself, its fractured light charging the climactic scene and final image. This is a model of how setting can be an active agent in the narrative.</comment>

This is a subtle but powerful escalation.</comment>

The final image of sorrow as a "lens" is a perfect encapsulation of this theme, earned entirely through the preceding events.</comment>
