The escalation is entirely internal but potent and fits the story's scope perfectly.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The theme of finding balance between past and future, decay and growth, is woven skillfully through the story’s central images: the ancient meteorite, decaying paper, and renewed flight. The piece achieves a poignant emotional aftereffect rooted in its specific, concrete particulars.</comment>

These line-level issues create minor drag and slightly blunt the impact of an otherwise clear and propulsive narrative.</comment>
