<question>2</question><grade>9.2</grade><comment>The story's structure is flawless for its length, operating under a clear Accumulative/Textural lens. The final image of the new spiral provides a perfect, cyclical cadence.</comment>

The environment directly constrains and enables Elara's actions, and the final chaotic image is a direct, causal result of its nature being "unmade."</comment>

This moment of thematic summary undercuts the subtext, a significant misstep that prevents the story from reaching the highest tier of literary craft.</comment>
