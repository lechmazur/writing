# Editorial Evaluation

## Drive Lens Inference
**Accumulative/Textural** – The piece accumulates sensory and symbolic layers, building toward a conceptual repricing in which the protagonist discovers time's texture through renunciation rather than possession. The "impossible choice" functions as a thematic loom rather than a causal fork.

## Closure Unit
**Conceptual Repricing (CR)** – The protagonist reframes his quest: instead of descending to grasp time's essence, he surrenders possession and receives whispered insight. The rope remains intact, the choice transforms from barrier to revelation, and the final image ("compass forged from impossible choices") reweights prior material with felt cost (he abandons the physical descent he pursued).
