<question>2</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>The narrative follows a clear Accumulative structure, building sensory pressure to a release point. The closure is a Resonant Reconfiguration (the postcard changing from singed to soft), which works lyrically. <question>3</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The "gargantuan shell" is an excellent, imaginative setting that actively shapes the narrative through the "pinhole camera" projections. The sensory details ("tasted of brine," "swirls of violet and gold") are vivid and establish a strong, cohesive atmosphere.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>6.0</grade><comment>The stakes (sanity/processing grief) are clear, and the "relentless hum" of the city provides good context for the pressure. The tension deflates rather than snapping, leading to a gentle but low-stakes resolution.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>5.6</grade><comment>The theme is legible but heavily "told" through explicit labels like "blossom armistice" and "photosynthesize pain into beauty." The resonance relies on these stated concepts rather than subtextual depth.</comment>
