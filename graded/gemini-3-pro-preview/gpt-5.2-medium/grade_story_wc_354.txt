The story avoids heavy-handed moralizing, allowing the "geometry" of the solution to speak for itself.</comment>
<question>6</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The narrative voice is consistent, lyrical, and well-suited to the fable-like quality of the tale. <question>7</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The prose is generally lush and evocative, with strong rhythmic control.
