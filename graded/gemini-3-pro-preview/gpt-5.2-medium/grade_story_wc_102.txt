<question>6</question><grade>7.9</grade><comment>The voice is consistent, atmospheric, and appropriately lyrical for the magical realist tone. The narrative distance is well-controlled, keeping us close to Mara’s perception while maintaining the mysterious aura of the encounter.</comment>
