<question>4</question><grade>4.6</grade><comment>The dominant stake (reviving the ghost town) faces no meaningful opposition or complication—the tuner's method works exactly as intended without setback or cost. <question>5</question><grade>5.8</grade><comment>The theme of balance between "stone and spirit" emerges through the restoration process and resonates in the final image of children playing over cobblestones. However, the theme tends toward abstraction ("shared balance") without exploring complexity or counter-arguments to the tuner's vision.</comment>

<question>6</question><grade>5.4</grade><comment>The prose maintains a consistent mystical register but lacks a distinct perceptual signature—the narration could belong to any omniscient observer rather than reflecting the tuner's specific consciousness.
