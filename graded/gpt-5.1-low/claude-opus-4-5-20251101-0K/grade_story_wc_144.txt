<question>2</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The causal chain is clean: merchant’s request → diagnosis → repair under time pressure → successful launch → quiet reaffirmation of purpose. Closure lands as an earned stasis/charged poise moment when she sends the drone off and returns to her shop, but the decisive change is modest. Because the cost and consequence remain small and largely untested, the structural impact feels solid rather than compelling.</comment>
<question>3</question><grade>8.3</grade><comment>The misty rail depot, abandoned locomotives, and converted cargo car are vivid and atmospherically rich without becoming cluttered. Setting is filtered through Elara’s non-visual senses (sound, moisture, warmth), which makes the environment feel lived-in and specific to her perception. The water basin and fog both tie into her methods and the story’s stakes (navigation in mist), giving the world-building thematic coherence. It stops short of fully constraining her choices, but it strongly charges tone and method.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>6.3</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether she can repair the drone before the ship must sail—is clear and tracked via the noon deadline and harbor bells. However, pressure never meaningfully escalates beyond the stated time constraint; each technical step proceeds smoothly with no serious setback or forced change of tactic. Elara’s calm competence keeps tension low, and there’s no reversal that threatens failure or demands a trade-off. As a result, the conflict is functional but relatively gentle for the intended prestige tier.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>Themes of repair, resurrection, and reinterpreting damage are woven through Elara’s blindness, her methods, and the drone’s purpose, creating a coherent conceptual spine. That said, the final paragraph leans into explicit thematic summary (“The world was full of damage, but it was also full of messages”) that edges toward statement rather than pure image. The piece invites some reflection but resolves its ideas a bit tidily.</comment>

Prose maintains a poised, almost meditative distance that suits Elara’s “magnetic calm” while still conveying technical process clearly. Dialogue is minimal but serviceable. There is occasional explanatory phrasing, yet overall the narrative stance feels controlled and character-specific.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The line-level writing is polished, with varied sentence rhythm and generally efficient, concrete detail.
