<question>2</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The causal structure is clean: eavesdropping → pattern recognition → realization of a purge → commitment to smuggle the warning, all contained in one coherent scene. The one constraint is that the “coup in three days” clock and her concrete plan surface late, so the sense of narrowed options is more conceptual than viscerally felt.</comment>
<question>3</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>The observatory is vivid and purposeful: cobalt dome, autumn wind, lamplight, and glass all mirror the story’s focus on looking versus listening. The eternal candle and silk function as micro-settings/tools that directly shape her tactics and inflect the closing image. Environmental and atmospheric details are consistently filtered through Mira’s priorities, avoiding generic description. The setting constrains and enables spying in plausible ways, and the final return to flame and leaves gives the world a charged, lived-in feel.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.3</grade><comment>The dominant stake—exposing or thwarting a coup/purge—is clear and raised effectively when Mira realizes the breadth of the conspiracy. Escalation occurs mainly through deepening knowledge rather than reversals, culminating in her decision to act and the three-day deadline. However, much of the pressure remains intellectual; we rarely see concrete peril, loss, or sacrifice in the present moment that would sharpen the cost of her choice. The conflict is solid and coherent but could use one more on-page squeeze that tests her resolve under immediate risk.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>Themes of unseen labor, the power of listening, and truth encoded in marginalized crafts emerge organically from Mira’s actions and the observatory’s dynamics. The piece nudges toward explicitness in lines like “threading wisdom through the disguise of foolishness,” which verge on thesis statements but stop short of outright moralizing. Overall, the thematic architecture is strong, if a bit neatly articulated.</comment>
