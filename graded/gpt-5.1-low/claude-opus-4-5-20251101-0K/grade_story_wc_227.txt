<question>3</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The acid sea, copper pontoons, and gear-cathedral workshop are vivid and consistently filtered through Vera’s attention. The final image of a gold-veined workshop drifting through emerald mist is powerful and charged by prior detail. A slight limitation is that the acid sea threatens but never quite constrains a tactical choice beyond mood and metaphor.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Vera can transform her broken past into something meaningfully integrated before literal/psychic dissolution—is legible and escalates as she moves to ever-deeper wounds. The workshop lurching, gears self-activating, and the increasingly painful memories provide a clear tightening. Agency is centered in her choice to continue the narration and to match the deepest pockmark to her daughter, paying emotional cost to complete the pattern. The limitation is that external danger (the acid sea, structural collapse) remains more atmospheric than concretely constraining her options in the moment.</comment>

The emotional aftereffect is still genuine and earned.</comment>
