<question>5</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>Theme—about thresholds where rational explanation fails, the cost and allure of yielding to mystery, and memory as a sensory key—is woven through concrete particulars (petrichor as “keys,” the compass’s singular purpose, Vennard ceasing to seek a way back).
