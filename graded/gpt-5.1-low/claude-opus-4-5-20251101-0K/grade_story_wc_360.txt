The setting constrains tone and theme more than tactical choices, but it strongly charges the closure.</comment>

The cosmic backdrop of disappearing stars gestures toward larger external stakes, yet it doesn’t materially tighten her options in-scene beyond a generalized “running out of time.” As a result the conflict feels resonant but low-pressure, more contemplative than urgent.</comment>

However, several lines verge on explicit thesis-statement (“Emotion was not a formula to be solved but a harmony to be discovered”), slightly flattening subtext into statement. Combined with relatively soft on-page pressure, the theme leans a bit didactic where it could have trusted image more.</comment>
