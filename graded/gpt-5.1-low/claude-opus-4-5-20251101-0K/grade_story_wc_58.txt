Overall, atmosphere is rich and purposeful without bogging down momentum.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The central stake—whether Elara can reconfigure her relationship to her brother’s absence and her own work—appears early and stays coherent. However, conflict remains relatively smooth: there’s little on-page resistance, backsliding, or concrete alternative she nearly chooses and rejects. The escalation reads as a steady deepening rather than a sharp tightening, which works but leaves some potential tension on the table.</comment>

The piece leaves a resonant emotional aftereffect, though a slightly less explicit final articulation would raise its literary subtlety.</comment>
