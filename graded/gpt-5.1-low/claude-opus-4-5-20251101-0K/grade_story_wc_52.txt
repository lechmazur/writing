<question>5</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>Themes of grief as transformation, containment, and crystallization emerge coherently through the lions, wood, workshop, and teaching. The final acceptance of “perhaps this was enough” is resonant and earned by prior particulars. The piece balances lyric subtlety with a bit of overt didacticism, keeping it in the strong-but-not-exceptional range.</comment>

The story’s ingenuity lies more in its precise synthesis of elements than in any single startlingly original turn.</comment>
