Yet escalation is weakened by the muddled, multi-option climax—poison aerosol vs. psychological unraveling vs. physical shove—so the exact nature of the price and outwitting feels less sharply etched than it could. Conflict resolves decisively but not with maximal clarity or layered consequence.</comment>

However, the piece sometimes states theme outright (“she knew vulnerability was power”) rather than letting it emerge fully from scene and image, edging toward didacticism. The final image of her standing under stars with fears as “allies” is resonant but a bit too neatly articulated.</comment>
