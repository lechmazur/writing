<question>4</question><grade>6.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—facilitating a “flawless goodbye” through father–daughter reunification—is clear and present from early on, and Elara’s actions are oriented around it. The piece leans toward sentimentality, with limited sense that anything vitally true is being risked or left unresolved.</comment>
