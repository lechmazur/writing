<question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether the island can retain its cultural memory—is well signaled and emotionally legible. Yet opposition is soft: the shadows never directly contest her, the Guardian offers no serious resistance, and there’s no moment where a plausible path is foreclosed with concrete loss.
