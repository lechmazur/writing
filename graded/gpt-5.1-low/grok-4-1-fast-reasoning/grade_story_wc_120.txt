The piece still invites reflection on how we compose our own pasts, but with a touch more neatness than mystery.</comment>

That said, figurative language is nearly continuous, occasionally flirting with purple and slightly flattening impact through saturation. Trimming a few redundant intensifiers and abstractions would sharpen the already strong line-level craft.</comment>

Emotional territory (nostalgia, longing for the past) is familiar, but the execution—the specific tools and images—refreshes it.
