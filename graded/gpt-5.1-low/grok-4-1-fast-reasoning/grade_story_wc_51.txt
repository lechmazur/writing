The piece avoids overt moralizing and allows conceptual resonance around what it means to carry others’ griefs. <question>8</question><grade>8.7</grade><comment>The core imaginative apparatus—a fractal weaver weighing human burdens in a bronze grotto, using a kerosene burner and starlight to calcify infinite patterns—is striking and distinctive. The turn where being trapped in recursion leads to a deliberate, ancestral calcification method is an elegant conceptual escalation.
