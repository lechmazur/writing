<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether beauty can be reawakened in a flawed, mechanized world—is clear, and pressure rises as Elarion commits to activating the device and the egg begins to crack. <question>5</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The theme of “flawed genesis”—beauty as inherently compromised and entangled with ruin—is tightly woven through the egg, the malformed chick, and the fusion of organic and mechanical motifs. The ending reweights prior material: beauty’s return is neither redemptive nor futile but a fragile, sorrow-tinged persistence that fits the prince’s earlier bitterness. Rhythm remains mostly controlled, but a leaner line in climactic beats would sharpen the closure image and stakes.</comment>
