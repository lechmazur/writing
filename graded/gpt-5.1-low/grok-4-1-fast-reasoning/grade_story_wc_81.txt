<question>5</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>Theme emerges coherently from events and images: truth versus myth, human hubris versus divine blame, and knowledge as a non-literal light (“In the blackout's heart, light was reborn—not of lanterns, but insight”). Overall, the piece offers a clear, resonant answer to its central question without collapsing into pure moralizing.</comment>
