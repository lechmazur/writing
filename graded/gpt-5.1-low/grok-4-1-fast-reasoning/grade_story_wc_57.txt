The only thing keeping this just under the very top band is that the dock and weather rarely impose hard constraints on action; they’re more atmospheric than tactical.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>The dominant stake—preserving fragile, encoded knowledge before it’s lost—is thematically potent but plays out with relatively low on-page resistance. There is a meaningful deepening as Elias penetrates toward the “master weave” and commits to teaching the boy, narrowing his path to a life of stewardship. However, escalation feels mostly linear and contemplative rather than pressured; no real option is foreclosed beyond the abstract fear of forgetting.</comment>

The thematic aftertaste is strong and coherent, but a hair too explained to enter the highest tier.</comment>
