<question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—reclaiming agency and escaping the lab’s entanglement—is clear early and escalates through Elara’s increasingly intense, painful drumming. Still, the path to success encounters little concrete opposition or complication; each escalation leads fairly directly to a positive outcome, so the risk of failure never feels sharply narrowed. Emotional stakes are vivid, but practical jeopardy and trade-offs could be more rigorously dramatized.</comment>

Some lines verge on explicit thematic statement (“Memories flowed freely now, no longer chains but wings”), softening subtlety a touch. Nonetheless, the piece resists neat moralizing and instead leaves a resonant sense of cost alongside liberation.</comment>
