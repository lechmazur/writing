At times the imagery verges on lush for its own sake, but it largely remains purposeful and filtered through Elias’s chess- and thought-focused worldview. <question>4</question><grade>6.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—awakening second thoughts under an authoritarian Council—is clear, but conflict remains mostly one-sided: Elias devises and executes his plan without resistance. The ending image—amplified quiet now “whispering of change”—offers a resonant, non-literal answer to the central question without full moralizing. More tension between the value and danger of doubt (e.g., what questioning might cost specific people) could add complexity.</comment>
