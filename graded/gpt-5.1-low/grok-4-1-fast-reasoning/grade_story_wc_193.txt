There’s no dialogue to stress-test voice under direct interaction, but the narrative stance itself remains coherent.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>6.6</grade><comment>The prose offers many striking images and a strong rhythmic flow; several sentences carry multiple functions (worldbuilding, mood, and character at once). <question>8</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The synthesis—a pyramid-scheme survivor in a bioluminescent mushroom farm during an era when concepts become physical, using a submarine bolt and quantum texts to turn trauma-pressure into communal diamonds—is genuinely fresh. While the underlying emotional arc (turning personal ruin into altruistic purpose) is familiar, the execution through this particular constellation of elements is memorable. A bit more unpredictability or moral complication could push it into truly exceptional territory.</comment>
