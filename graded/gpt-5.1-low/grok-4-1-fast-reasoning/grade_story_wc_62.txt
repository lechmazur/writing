<question>5</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The thematic throughline—grief as communal, interwoven, and transformative rather than isolated—is consistently embodied in concrete particulars: the ribbon’s weave, overlapping poems, migration loops, and the distribution of sorrow across threads.
