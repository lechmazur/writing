The final image of a lighter, brighter greenhouse with possibility “taking root” charges the closure in a way that is inseparable from place. <question>4</question><grade>6.6</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Alaric will remain a passive “puppet” of his past or align himself with possibility—is present and conceptually interesting. However, the conflict remains largely internal and unopposed; he doesn’t seriously contemplate refusal, and no competing value or fear pushes back on his emerging allegiance. The result is a graceful deepening rather than a felt test of commitment with a clear price.</comment>

The resonance is genuine but somewhat over-declared rather than fully earned through implication.</comment>
