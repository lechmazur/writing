<question>3</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The abandoned carnival is vividly rendered—the creaking Ferris wheel, faded banners, redemption booth—and it meaningfully shapes tactics (labyrinthine layout for evasion, hidden bench, floorboards concealing the vault). However, sensory range stays mostly visual and atmospheric rather than fully tactile under stress, and the setting could constrain physical choice more tightly in the climax. Overall it’s an asset and feels integrated rather than touristic.</comment>

The chase and collapse read more like expected beats than escalating dilemmas. Conflict is coherent and sustained but stops short of a truly costly test.</comment>
