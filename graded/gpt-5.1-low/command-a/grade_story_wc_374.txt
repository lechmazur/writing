It’s strong atmosphere with one meaningful structural function, not yet a full tactical constraint.</comment>

<question>8</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The constellation of ideas—the emergent emotional compass tasted on the tongue, sentient forests holding elections, people who carry weather, and the “terrestrially cosmic” bridge of aurora—is genuinely fresh.
