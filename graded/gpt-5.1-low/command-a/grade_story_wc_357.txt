<question>4</question><grade>5.5</grade><comment>Stakes are large in theory—planetary resilience and a dying core—but the on-page conflict is soft: Elara encounters no real opposition, moral dilemma, or meaningful risk. <question>5</question><grade>6.0</grade><comment>Themes of resilience, memory, and balance between chaos and life are coherent and organically connected to the world’s rules (echoes stored in plants, inversion, accelerated growth). Complexity is limited: Elara’s newfound purpose faces no counter-pressure or ambiguity on the page.</comment>
