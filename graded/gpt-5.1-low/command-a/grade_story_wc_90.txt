<question>4</question><grade>6.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—unlocking a dimension key to humanity’s survival—is announced strongly, but the on-page conflict is gentle and mostly internal. Shadow work is described more as something she has already done than a present-tense struggle that could cost her something now. As a result, escalation is minimal and the stakes feel more conceptual than viscerally tested.</comment>

The ending offers a resonant answer in image (final stitch, awakened dimension, recycled ship as resilience), but that resonance is somewhat blunted by prior exposition. The piece invites reflection, just not with the subtlety a top-market venue typically demands.</comment>
