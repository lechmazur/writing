Sensory detail is selective and attuned to Elara’s obsessions. While the setting doesn’t strongly constrain physical options, it meaningfully shapes the story’s emotional and conceptual field.</comment>

Her mapping intensifies, yet there’s little sense of what she might lose by failing, or of competing imperatives that make her choice costly. The escalation reads more as deepened attention than sharpened conflict, leaving tension relatively low for a piece aiming at elite venues.</comment>

The one slight weakness is a touch of explicitness in the last sentences (“she had preserved more than the echo of lost songs—she had preserved herself”), which edges toward telling rather than fully trusting the image. Still, the thematic integration is strong and resonant.</comment>
