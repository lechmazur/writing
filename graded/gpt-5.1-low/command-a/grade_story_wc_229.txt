The setting participates in the story’s meaning by mirroring internal change—heat wave ending, cool tenderness rising—more than by constraining physical choices. It stops just short of being structurally indispensable but significantly elevates mood and resonance.</comment>

The beckoning and subsequent following are turning points, yet they unfold without real doubt or pushback. As a result, tension is gentle and meditative rather than truly pressurized.</comment>

The woman’s direct articulation of the core line, and its restatement in the ending, edge into didacticism. The piece still leaves a mild afterglow of reflection, but with less ambiguity or complexity than a top-tier literary venue would demand.</comment>

Line-level craft is solid enough for competitive consideration, though not yet dazzling.</comment>
<question>8</question><grade>6.9</grade><comment>The combination of a lighthouse setting with a quasi-mystical object tied to “vibrations of intent” and scented guidance offers a gentle twist on familiar introspective fantasy. Yet the arc—a guide figure delivering a compact lesson, leading to an immediate inner resolution—is structurally conventional. The piece is thoughtfully composed but could take more risks in either form, consequence, or psychological complexity to feel truly distinctive at a top-tier level.</comment>
