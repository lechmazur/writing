Strong atmosphere and integration, but not yet at a level where setting actively pressures decisions.</comment>

The single major turn (activating the bloom) is resolved on the first attempt with almost no risk or sacrifice, so tension never tightens into a true test of her resolve.</comment>

Lines like “knowing she had done what was needed” and “The bloom of hush would endure” feel somewhat declarative, smoothing away ambiguity. Subtext about the cost or danger of silence versus chaos is only lightly implied instead of explored.</comment>
