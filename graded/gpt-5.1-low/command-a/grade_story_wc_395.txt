The old terminal and patch function more as plot tools than as fully charged setting elements. Atmosphere is serviceable but not vividly immersive.</comment>

There is little rhythmic daring or distinct perceptual signature, and the lack of dialogue limits opportunities for textural variation. Competent but not distinctive.</comment>

<question>8</question><grade>6.6</grade><comment>The fusion of a corporate memory keeper, social network archives, and a vanished spaceship drawn to extra-galactic coordinates offers a reasonably fresh configuration of familiar SF tropes.
