<question>5</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The theme of honoring absence rather than erasing it is beautifully embedded in concrete particulars: missing chairs, blank pages, deliberate gaps in the rope’s weave, and the final integration of the treaty’s loss. The piece still invites genuine reflection beyond its plot, just with a touch more explicitness than needed.</comment>
