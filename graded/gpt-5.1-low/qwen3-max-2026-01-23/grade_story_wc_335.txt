<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Elara will continue to reject or engage in a path that might reverse her exile—is clear and stable. The decisive turn is internal and quiet; there is little sense of external jeopardy or immediate cost beyond emotional risk, which fits the mode but keeps intensity at a mid-high level rather than urgent. Still, the narrowing from refusal to active engagement reads earned and coherent.</comment>

The piece has genuine resonance but edges toward didacticism at moments.</comment>
