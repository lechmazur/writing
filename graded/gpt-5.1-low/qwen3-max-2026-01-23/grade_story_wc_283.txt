Objects-as-setting—the buttons, the jar, the hourglasses—are not mere decor but the tools through which she reads and alters the situation. Description occasionally verges on lushness, but it remains purposeful and strongly supports the ending image.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether the prophesied collapse remains fate or becomes a collectively-altered future—is clear and present from early on. The resonance is real but would be stronger with slightly less explicit statement.</comment>
