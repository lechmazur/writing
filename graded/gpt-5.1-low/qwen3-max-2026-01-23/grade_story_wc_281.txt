The cost is present but subtle, residing mainly in Elara’s willingness to let go of explanatory control.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>Themes of embodied wisdom, empathy versus proof, and art as a conduit for grief are threaded coherently through somatic imagery, light, and the willow motif.
