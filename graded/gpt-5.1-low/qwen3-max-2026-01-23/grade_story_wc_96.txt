Still, the internal conflict and the decision to accept transformation while committing to the rebuild provide a credible, if quiet, narrowing of options.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>Themes of transformation, collective resistance, and beauty-from-brokenness are woven through concrete particulars—the library, the prison backstory, the seed, the personas. The lyricism occasionally leans close to uniform register across narrative and dialogue, but rhythm and diction are controlled enough that it reads as an intentional, coherent aesthetic.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>8.3</grade><comment>Line to line, the prose is polished, with good syntactic variety and high information density: almost every sentence advances atmosphere, character, or theme. Still, there are no major loops, mixed metaphors, or rhythm failures that impede clarity or momentum.</comment>
