<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The central stake—whether Elara can transform her unresolved grief into understanding—is established early and escalates through the ritual and vision, culminating in a clear internal shift. The story succeeds as quiet pressure rather than sharp tension, which is apt but keeps stakes from feeling fully acute.</comment>
