The closure as Earned Stasis/Resonant Reconfiguration lands solidly on the page.</comment>
<question>3</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The gargantuan shell, its pearlescent nacre, the off-key wind, and the dunes form a highly specific, atmospheric micro-world that feels lived-in and consequential. The environment doesn’t just decorate; it constrains options (self-exile in the shell) and directly shapes the climax as the shell becomes a luminous archive of his sorrow. The recurring postcard and galaxy patterns further charge the final image, integrating object and setting.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—finding a livable relationship to endless insomnia and longing—is clear and emotionally credible, and the pressure subtly tightens through repetition toward the night when the shell answers him. However, the key turn (the shell’s resonance and light) is more an external “grace” responding to his accumulated endurance than a new, risky choice; he is doing what he has always done when the breakthrough occurs. The cost is strongly implied (centuries of ache), but moment-to-moment agency at the climax is relatively passive.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.6</grade><comment>Themes of transforming pain into beauty, accepting rather than resisting darkness, and the idea of a “blossom armistice” emerge organically from recurring images (photosynthesis, night-blooming cereus, reef-shadow joy, the shell as archive). The final state—dancing with the darkness instead of fighting it—reweights all prior material without lapsing into moralizing. A few abstract phrases edge toward thematic statement, but they remain grounded enough in specific imagery not to feel didactic.</comment>
<question>6</question><grade>8.7</grade><comment>The third-person close voice is consistently lyric, with a distinctive lexicon (“reef-shadow joy,” “blossom armistice,” “shyly unstoppable”) that matches the timeless, fable-like frame. Rhythm and syntax are controlled; the prose modulates gently at the climax so the illumination feels like a genuine cadence shift rather than just another ornate paragraph. There is no POV slippage or explanatory “helper narrator” tone undermining the intimacy.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The line-level writing is lush but generally precise, with images doing multiple jobs (worldbuilding, emotion, theme) and minimal scaffolding or redundancy.
