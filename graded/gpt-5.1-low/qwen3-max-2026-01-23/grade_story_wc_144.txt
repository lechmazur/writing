The tone and atmosphere are cohesive, with weather, industrial sound, and water all reinforcing the contemplative, liminal mood. A slight excess of descriptive language edges toward ornament in places but does not break clarity or function.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>6.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—repair the drone before the Celestial Dawn departs—is clearly signaled, and the harbor bell and anchor movement offer a nominal escalation. Internal and external pressures thus remain relatively static, so the final success, while satisfying, lacks a strong sense of narrowing options or paid price.</comment>
