<question>5</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>Themes of awe vs explanation, truth vs safety, and surrender vs control are coherent and arise from concrete particulars (the compass’s refusal of north, the still lagoon, her renunciation of collection). Here, safety lies in surrender.”) and the last line (“wonder begins precisely where explanation ends”) slide into explicit moralizing that states the lesson rather than fully trusting image and action.
