<question>5</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The throughline—brokenness as a site for more light, and abandonment as spacious release rather than defeat—emerges organically from repeated particulars (bookmark cracks, fracture, thaw, scrap bin, dome). Rich thematic coherence is present, but the wisdom is a shade too spelled out for top-tier subtlety.</comment>
