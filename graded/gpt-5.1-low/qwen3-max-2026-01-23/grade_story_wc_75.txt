<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether the community’s ancestral baking ritual survives or ossifies into loss—is clearly signaled and paid off, and there is a meaningful tightening when Elara realizes the beekeeper-centric story is wrong and chooses to act immediately.
