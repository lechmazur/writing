<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—obtaining the sundial lid and confronting the unknown—is clear, and tension does rise when the shadow appears and Lirien initiates the risky trade. The conceptual warning (“time is not what you seek. It is what seeks you.”) deepens stakes thematically but doesn’t translate into concrete on-page cost. <question>8</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The combination of a chivalrous thief, secrets-as-chess-pieces, a wrong sky, and time as an antagonist concept gives this a fresh angle on familiar “relic in a tower” fantasy.
