<question>4</question><grade>7.3</grade><comment>The dominant stake—understanding what happened before it kills or assimilates him, and escaping to warn others—is clear and sharpened by the realization that the city is “rapidly adapting.” Pressure escalates convincingly as the environment shifts from uncanny to actively antagonistic, culminating in the labyrinth reconfiguring as he runs. However, his agency is somewhat limited to investigate-then-run, with few concrete tactical choices or trade-offs on the page.
