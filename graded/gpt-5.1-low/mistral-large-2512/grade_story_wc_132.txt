Detail is selective and purposeful rather than touristic. The environment could shape tactical choices a bit more strongly, but it already does solid work in mood and theme.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The central stake—whether Mara will cling to control or accept a non-human agency she doesn’t command—is present early and deepens as the sapling’s metamorphosis intensifies. Escalation is more internal than external; there is no overt danger or heavy cost beyond psychic risk, which keeps tension at a quiet but sustained level. The resolution through endurance/surrender is earned, if not searingly fraught.</comment>

However, lines like “It needed her to *let go*” and “reconstruct the world in her image” edge toward explicit statement, slightly dulling subtextual power. The piece still invites reflection beyond the scene, but with a bit of on-the-nose articulation.</comment>

Overall, the story shows thoughtful synthesis and a particular sensibility, though it stops short of formally or conceptually daring territory.</comment>
