The terrace and its objects charge the final image—especially the bench grain running straight and the prism set down—making the setting integral to the closure. Sensory detail is mostly visual but used with precision and thematic purpose.</comment>

<question>5</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>Motifs of thresholds, storms, prisms, and wood grain interlock to explore sacred versus dangerous crossings and the costs of necessary distance in relationships. However, the line “He understood, then, why some thresholds felt sacred” edges toward explicit thesis and slightly flattens what has been mostly subtextual. Despite this, the ending offers a resonant, non-literal answer to the initial question about thresholds and loss.</comment>

The voice sustains both atmosphere and interiority without resorting to explanation-heavy exposition.</comment>
