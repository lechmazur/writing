The archives’ atmosphere as a “living thing” is economically but effectively established. While the setting doesn’t heavily constrain physical tactics, it clearly charges the final images.</comment>

The specter’s warning (“bridges can be burned”) hints at future cost but doesn’t force an on-page decision. The result is compelling but closer to an elegant unveiling than a tightened crucible.</comment>
