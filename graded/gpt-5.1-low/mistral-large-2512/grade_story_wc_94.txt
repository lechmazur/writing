<question>3</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>The observatory above the clouds in a lightning storm is striking and sustained; weather, light, and architecture are all integrated with Elias’s perception rather than feeling like postcard description. <question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake is internal—how Elias will position himself ethically and artistically toward the scars/photo—and that is signaled early and followed through. The final renunciation (accepting that some maps must be carried, not read) is a meaningful stance but the visible cost and narrowing of options are understated, in part because no external alternative path is dramatized.
