<question>4</question><grade>6.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether to uncover a crushing, multiversal truth or preserve ignorance—is stated clearly and sharpened by the arrival of the scarred Kael and the shard-console activation. Still, the conflict remains largely conceptual; we don’t see concrete consequences or sacrifices tied to either option, and the protagonist’s agency at the end is muted by the off-page decision.
