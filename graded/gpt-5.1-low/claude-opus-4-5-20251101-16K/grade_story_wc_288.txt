<question>3</question><grade>8.6</grade><comment>The tidal library above the clouds, the atomically reflective sundial, and the humming stone circle are vivid, specific, and consistently filtered through Elara’s perspective. World rules feel internally consistent for a lyrical speculative piece.</comment>

The escalation is primarily internal and thematic, culminating in her absorbing the ghosts and claiming the keeper role, which forecloses the path of release. Stakes stay a bit abstract and could benefit from one more concrete, on-page cost or threatened failure.</comment>
