Beyond this, opposition is largely existential rather than dramatized, so escalation feels more like a smooth accumulation than a ratcheting of tension. The final choice to hang the tapestry and “feed” her own feelings to the wall is affecting, but the sense of risk or trade-off is muted.</comment>
