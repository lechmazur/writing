The final image of her dancing under those same stars closes the loop, charging the garden as both prison and temple. Detail is selective and purposeful rather than touristic, with tools and flora tightly bound to theme and action.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The stakes—memory versus state-mandated erasure—are conceptually high and clearly signaled early, and Vesna’s choice to tattoo Cael knowing he will return to the Sovereignty defines the central risk.
