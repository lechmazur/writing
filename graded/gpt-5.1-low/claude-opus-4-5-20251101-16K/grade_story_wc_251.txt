The psychic distance is well-managed, slipping easily between external scene and interior reflection without confusion, and there is no POV leakage or jarring register shift.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>Sentence-level craft is polished: varied rhythms, strong verbs, and generally high information density with images doing thematic and emotional work simultaneously.
