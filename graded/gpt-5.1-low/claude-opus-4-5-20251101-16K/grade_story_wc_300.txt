While the environment doesn’t tightly constrain her physical options, it decisively charges the discovery of the shell and the sacred tenor of her realization. This is rich, economical atmosphere rather than postcard description.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake is Vera’s willingness to dismantle her own emotional bunkers, and the story escalates by moving from generalized observation of others’ fears to intimate confessions to her personal reckoning. Pressure is primarily internal and quiet, so the piece never reaches a strong sense of jeopardy, but the cost of vulnerability is nonetheless legible. The escalation is effective, if gentle, for this mode.</comment>

Despite that explicitness, the emotional aftereffect is genuine and lingers.</comment>
