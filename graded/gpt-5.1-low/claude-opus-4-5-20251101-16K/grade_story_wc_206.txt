<question>4</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The central stake—whether wonder and “light” can be reclaimed from midnight’s theft—is clear from early on, and pressure coheres as midnight manifests and the stone begins to wilt. The piece invites reflection but doesn’t fully embrace ambiguity or complication.</comment>
