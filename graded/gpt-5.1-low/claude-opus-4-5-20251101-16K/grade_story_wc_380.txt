<question>4</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Mira can locate and reclaim lost memories in a world where minds erase them—emerges early and tightens as she follows gravity pockets and uncovers the journal. A few lines do skate close to explicit thesis (“nothing truly disappeared”), but the resonance predominately arises from image and action rather than stated moral.</comment>
