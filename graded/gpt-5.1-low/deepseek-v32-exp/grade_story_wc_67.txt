<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether and how Elara can transform hurt into a workable, honest memory—remains clear throughout. However, the conflict is very one-sided; there’s little genuine sense she might refuse or abort the ritual, so tension never fully peaks. The stakes feel intimate and real but not sharply contested on the page.</comment>
