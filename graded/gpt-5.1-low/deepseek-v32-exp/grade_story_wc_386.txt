There is resonance, but less ambiguity or friction than top-tier literary work tends to sustain.</comment>

Rhythm is competent but not yet striking or risk-taking.</comment>

Many pivotal beats—particularly the investigation—are told in compressed summary, reducing line-level vividness and momentum. The figurative language is coherent but safe, without the sharp, surprising precision expected at a top literary level.</comment>
