<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stakes—preventing cosmic-induced chaos and averting community violence—are signaled early and tightened by the explicit prohibition against unauthorized broadcasts. There is resonance, but complexity and ambiguity are kept at a relatively safe, familiar level.</comment>
