However, some of the language (“the crushing weight of a love so fierce it demanded sacrifice”) edges toward generalized abstraction, smoothing over what could be messier, more particular moral ambiguity. The ending resonates, but in a relatively tidy way that feels more illustrative than deeply interrogative.</comment>

Overall, the premise-feel is distinctive, but the execution doesn’t yet fully exploit its most radical implications.</comment>
