Overall, specificity and tone are strong even if environmental pressure is limited.</comment>

The “unfastening” moment marks a structural turn but not an escalation of pressure; it’s a procedure, not a crisis. There is some tendency toward similar sentence cadences and abstract language, but it does not significantly harm clarity or stance.</comment>

Overall, the prose is competent and sometimes pleasing, but not yet razor-tight or maximally efficient.</comment>
<question>8</question><grade>7.3</grade><comment>The concept of a “between-worlds translator” using physical “anchors of intuition” to unearth forgotten acts in a magical market is fresh and appealing. The ingenuity lies more in the worldbuilding motifs than in the conflict design or final reconfiguration.</comment>
