<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The central stake—whether Elara can overcome creative paralysis after public humiliation—is clear and emotionally grounded. As a result, escalation feels more like a gentle incline than a tightening spiral, and the price she pays is mostly retrospective sorrow rather than an on-page trade-off.</comment>
