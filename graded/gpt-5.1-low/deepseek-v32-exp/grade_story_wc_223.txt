The final image of the gallery’s “luminous absence” and the now-potential chessboard demonstrates setting charging the closure. Escalation is modest, expressed through the progression of words she walks and the confrontation with the pawn-less chessboard, culminating in the choice to seek self-forgiveness. The emotional price is implied rather than sharply felt, which keeps tension at a measured, contemplative level.</comment>

However, several lines tilt into explicit thematic statement (“She realized that becoming the historian of small gestures meant…”), which undercuts some subtextual power. The ending is moving but edges toward neat moral resolution rather than lingering ambiguity.</comment>
