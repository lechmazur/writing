<question>4</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>The dominant stake—unmasking Theron and restoring the true queen—is clearly signaled and returns at the climax. The rune activation ends the conflict almost effortlessly, with little visible price beyond the usurper’s collapse.</comment>

The piece offers insight, but with a tidy moral rather than lingering ambiguity.</comment>

At times the density of imagery and abstraction (“cycles measured across the pause of shifting constellations,” “weight of authenticity”) slows momentum and edges toward purple. Tightening a few ornate lines, especially around thematic statements, would sharpen impact.</comment>

However, the macro-arc (patient schemer exposes false king; magic strips his stolen power; true queen returns) remains conventional and largely un-subverted.
