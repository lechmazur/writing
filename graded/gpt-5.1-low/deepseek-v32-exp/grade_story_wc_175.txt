Still, the setting’s specificity and recurrence do substantial work in anchoring mood and theme.</comment>

His final micro-choices (tying, waiting, untying, leaving) carry symbolic weight but minimal on-page consequence or trade-off. The result is a beautifully rendered moment that lacks a strong sense of escalation or test.</comment>

This slightly flattens what could otherwise be a more powerfully implicit thematic afterimage.</comment>

Psychic distance is managed cleanly; we stay inside Elian’s frame without head-hopping or confusing stance shifts. There is room for a more distinctive, character-specific verbal texture, but the current voice is polished and coherent.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The prose is generally elegant, with strong rhythm and well-chosen images that carry both atmosphere and meaning. The emotional arc—finding beauty in truthful acknowledgment of irrevocable loss—is familiar, but the specific ritual mechanics (knotting, wing as key, temporary architecture as signal) give it individuality.
