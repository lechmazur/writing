<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—can Elara break through emotional numbness and reconnect with music—is clear early and remains coherent. The cost (reopening pain) is there but could be sharpened with one more moment of real hesitation or potential refusal.</comment>

Despite that, the aftertaste is emotionally honest and conceptually coherent, not saccharine.</comment>

Rhythm and clarity at the closure beat remain intact, so these are minor rather than structural issues.</comment>

The piece takes a formal risk with its heavily lyric, motif-driven design and largely pulls it off without sacrificing clarity. Some thematic gestures (teacher-as-inspirational-figure) lean familiar, but the sensory and conceptual core stands out as genuinely distinctive.</comment>
