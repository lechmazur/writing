<question>5</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>Thematic intent around empathy, listening, and “cataloged silences” is clear and genuinely integrated—naming silences to reveal invisible ink is a strong metaphor. Complexity is limited: there’s little friction, ambiguity, or counter-pressure to complicate empathy as an uncomplicated good. The final vow and opened workshop resonate, but in a fairly tidy, didactic way.</comment>

At times the phrasing turns self-conscious or clunky (“his intuition intuitively descriptive”), revealing the machinery of style.
