The escalation is thus more contemplative than pressured, effective but not especially tense.</comment>

Overall, the voice feels controlled and intentional rather than generic.</comment>

The idea of “genuinely artificial” objects enabling real healing is a fresh inflection, even if the underlying emotional arc is classical.
