Internal conflict (her own grief) is asserted but not tested through hard choices; letting go feels narratively inevitable. As a result, escalation reads more as a graceful ascent through set pieces than as mounting pressure.</comment>

Still, the ending’s quiet reconfiguration of loss and beginning carries genuine resonance.</comment>

Some phrases are redundant or near-duplicative (“willingly perplexed” appears twice without fresh shading), and not every flourish earns its weight. The piece remains readable and evocative, but a more ruthless pruning would sharpen impact and momentum.</comment>
