The environment is not just backdrop; it shapes her methods and the central image of reestablished harmony. A slight excess of descriptive density in places keeps this just short of the top band.</comment>

There is some internal conflict implied by “haunted ambitions,” yet it never tightens into a choice with visible cost. As a result, escalation is minimal and tension remains low.</comment>

However, the final paragraphs edge close to explicit moralizing (“ambition, once haunted, could be guided toward compassion,” “every translation was a seed”), flattening some complexity.
