Moral and emotional aftereffect are present but somewhat tidy and didactic.</comment>

There are no POV slips or register jolts that would undercut control.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>6.9</grade><comment>The prose is generally clean, grammatically sound, and readable, with some attractive images (“lattice of sound,” “silver coin into the grotto’s pool”). <question>8</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The combination of a commuter ferry, spectral grotto, and time-weaving via radio resonance is genuinely fresh and conceptually appealing.
