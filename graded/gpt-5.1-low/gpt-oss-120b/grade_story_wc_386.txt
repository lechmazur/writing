Closure lands via a resonant reconfiguration: the globe shifts from relic to beacon, and her task reframes from solitary salvage to guiding many dreamers. The main limitation is that stakes and cost remain abstract, so the structural turn feels more conceptual than pressured.</comment>
<question>3</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The glacier shrine, candlelight, and borrowed starlight are vividly rendered and integrated into the action (e.g., starlight through fissures, candle flames catching it, the ice walls becoming a “choir of quiet light”). The melted snow globe functions as a recurring object that clearly charges the setting and the final image. The environment, however, rarely constrains her choices beyond a general cold, so it’s more atmospheric than tactical. Still, the specificity and consistency of the imagery create a strong, immersive atmosphere that supports theme and tone.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—releasing “pure essence” from the wreck and healing collective dreams—is clear but remains ethereal and largely uncontested. There is little sense of resistance or risk: the spirits do not actively push back, her attempts never fail or misfire, and solutions appear as linear realizations rather than pressured choices. The main tightening is the recognition that shards must be guided back via a final cameo, but this does not force a trade-off or visibly foreclose an option. As a result, escalation feels gentle and meditative rather than truly tense.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>Themes of salvaging purity from wreckage, collective hope, and truth as a delicate, reparative craft emerge organically from the dream-shard and melted-globe imagery. Occasionally the language flirts with abstract sermonizing (“truth, carefully woven, could expose the hidden beauty even in the deepest wreck”), but it doesn’t fully collapse into moralizing. The aftereffect is contemplative and cohesive, if not startlingly complex.</comment>

<question>7</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The prose is polished and often beautiful, with many sentences doing double duty for atmosphere and theme. Nonetheless, clarity is maintained, there are few redundancies, and the closing images land cleanly. Tightening a handful of figurative clusters would bring it closer to elite line-level control.</comment>

The emotional arc (salvaging purity from wreckage) is more familiar, but it’s rendered through idiosyncratic imagery rather than template beats. This is inventive and memorable, though not quite at the “inevitable and surprising” pinnacle.</comment>
