Crucially, the physical setting constrains options (hidden grooves, narrow crevice) and charges the final image of the canyon “listening” to the glass. A touch of descriptive repetition keeps this just below the very top band.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Elya can rebuild her art and sense of purpose after the fire—is stated, but it faces little on-page resistance. This produces a gentle deepening rather than a felt tightening or trade-off; no path is truly foreclosed. The resolution is more contemplative than pressured, leaving the conflict underdeveloped for elite-level publication.</comment>
