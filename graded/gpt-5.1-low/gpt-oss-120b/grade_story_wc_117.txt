As a result, escalation is gentle and tonal rather than truly testing her goal or values.</comment>

There is genuine emotional warmth, but the ending feels a bit too neatly inspirational, with limited ambiguity or counter-pressure. The conceptual repricing of the eclipse as a lesson in daily wonder is effective but somewhat on-the-nose.</comment>
