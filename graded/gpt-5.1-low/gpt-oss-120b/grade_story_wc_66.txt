<question>2</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>The story follows a clean accumulative arc: broadcast arrives, Mara transcribes, the city gathers, the song is played, and the city is changed. However, turns are gentle; events feel more like a sequence of images than tightening dilemmas, and no beat materially forecloses options. The one “tightening” is Mara deciding to act as conduit, but that decision is not framed as risky or costly. Conflict thus feels conceptual rather than tense, limiting escalation.</comment>
