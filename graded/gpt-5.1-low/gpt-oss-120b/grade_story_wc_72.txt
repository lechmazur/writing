The sensory palette leans heavily visual and metaphorical, with less textural or bodily grounding, and occasionally tips toward cataloguing. Still, the world rules feel coherent and the environment meaningfully shapes what can happen.</comment>

The “structural weakness” is used as a conduit, not as a genuine danger. As a result, escalation is more a gentle deepening of imagery than a tightening of pressure.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>6.2</grade><comment>Thematic intent—nuance softening ossified certainty—is clear and woven through recurring images (cracks, threads, mosaics, violet light). Because there’s little on-page resistance, wisdom comes as declaration rather than being wrestled out of conflict. The final image does reweight the premise conceptually, but with limited ambiguity or bite.</comment>

<question>7</question><grade>6.0</grade><comment>The prose is lush and often striking, but figurative language is nearly constant, creating a density that sometimes blurs rather than sharpens. <question>8</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The core constellation—a telepathic living statue in a forgotten underwater garden, threading memory through structural cracks to transform ossified myths—is genuinely inventive. Conceptually, however, the arc is familiar: revelation leading to enlightened harmony without real cost or complication. Strong originality at the level of imagery and premise, but the emotional and dramatic pattern remains comparatively safe.</comment>
