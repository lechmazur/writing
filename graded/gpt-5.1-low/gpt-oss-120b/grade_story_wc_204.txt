There’s no live alternative path or serious trade-off dramatized at the key beats, just successive escalations of spectacle. The result is a beautiful concept with low felt conflict and minimal visible price for Mara’s choices.</comment>

At times the story drifts into quasi-thesis language (“testimony that rustling awakenings can rewrite even the most immutable codes”), which flattens subtext into statement. Still, the thematic throughline is coherent and organically woven through the motif work.</comment>
