Sensory specificity is high and generally purposeful, although sometimes it leans into decorative lushness. <question>4</question><grade>6.3</grade><comment>The central stake—re-establishing a truthful, reciprocal bond with the garden—is clear but remains mostly conceptual and low-friction. His agency is continuous (choosing to polish, to promise, to kneel), yet consequences read as guaranteed reward rather than earned outcome under pressure. The result is a pleasing arc, but not a tense or hard-won one.</comment>
