<question>4</question><grade>6.3</grade><comment>Conflict is primarily internal and philosophical: whether to remain a guarding, waiting beast or to embrace uncertainty through the pendant and a new curatorial role. Waiting is framed as both punishment and promise, yet her decision to keep waiting while wearing the pendant doesn’t appreciably narrow options on the page. The stakes feel aesthetically interesting rather than urgent, which limits tension.</comment>
