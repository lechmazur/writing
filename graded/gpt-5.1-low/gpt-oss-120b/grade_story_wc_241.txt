<question>2</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The causal chain is clean: coin on the carved seat → panel opens → tunnel traversal via probabilistic scrawls → emergence and theorem-writing. Closure is present and legible, but lightly priced.</comment>

Environmental details like ratios mapping to library aisles show the world constraining choices rather than serving as static backdrop. The only limit is that weather and season color mood more than they alter tactics.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—Mara’s quest to reconcile probability and chance in the wake of her mother’s death—is conceptually high, but on the page the pressure remains gentle. The tunnel sequence presents no real obstacles, reversals, or costly trade-offs; each scrawl translates smoothly into progress. Her internal conflict (responsible widening of perspectives vs. inherited compulsion) is gestured at but never forces a painful choice or renunciation. As a result, escalation is minimal and the final theorem feels like an expected endpoint rather than a hard-won resolution.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The theme of “lucid contradictions” is woven consistently through gambler vs. professor, library vs. subway, certainty vs. chance, and culminates in the new theorem that unites them. However, the piece repeatedly names its core concept (“lucid contradictions,” “theoretically rebellious”) rather than fully embedding it in subtext, edging toward thesis statement. The ending earns some resonance but leans slightly didactic rather than letting images alone carry the insight.</comment>

With no dialogue, the piece avoids head-hopping and maintains a consistent perceptual signature.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The prose is generally clear, image-rich, and syntactically controlled, with many sentences doing double duty for atmosphere and worldbuilding. <question>8</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The fusion of gambling, academic probability, a secret subway beneath a rainwashed library, and the mica-lit lantern as both tool and symbol feels fresh and distinctive. The main limitation is that the narrative path is fairly straight, so the inventive ingredients don’t quite generate surprising turns. Still, the conceptual blend and object choices give the story a memorable, non-template identity.</comment>
