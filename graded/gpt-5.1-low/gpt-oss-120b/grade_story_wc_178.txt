Escalation is more a steady accumulation of descriptive reverence than a tightening of conflict. The thematic answer feels slightly too tidy and untroubled for elite literary subtlety.</comment>

Repeated phrases (“empirically ironic,” “dusky harmony”) border on incantatory rather than careless, but they do risk a hint of sameness. <question>8</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The synthesis of a Babylonian magic square, ritual astronomy, and a frugal, ledger-minded protagonist seeking a cosmic covenant is a fresh and appealing premise.
