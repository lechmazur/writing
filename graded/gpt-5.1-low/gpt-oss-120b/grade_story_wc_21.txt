<question>8</question><grade>9.0</grade><comment>The synthesis of elements—a civil-rights archivist on a zeppelin library, sand‑carvers reading beach debris, a retrocognitive diary encoded in a data-crystal lagoon—is strikingly inventive and cohesive.
