However, environment rarely constrains concrete choices; it shapes perception rather than forcing tactical adaptation. Still, the atmosphere is rich, specific, and integrated with the conceptual frame.</comment>

His agency consists mainly in choosing to attend and to interpret the eventual calm as acceptance, a choice with little visible cost. As a result, escalation is more tonal than structural and remains relatively flat.</comment>

However, the closing sentence that explicitly states “to distill complexity into essence was not to erase, but to honor each layer” edges toward essayistic moralizing. Thematic complexity is present but slightly over-articulated.</comment>
