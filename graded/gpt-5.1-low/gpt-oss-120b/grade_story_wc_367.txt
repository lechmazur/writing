<question>4</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>The dominant stake—using the ritual to avert or redirect prophetic storms and midwife a transformed dawn—is present, but its on-page jeopardy feels abstract. As a result, the pressure tightens somewhat but never truly bites.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>Themes of ending and rebirth, using “ancient shadows” to forge a hopeful dawn, and a bittersweet transition from an old world to a new one are coherently threaded through the imagery and climax. The thematic complexity is appealing but not yet at a startling or multi-layered depth.</comment>

Rhythm and syntax vary enough to keep the prose from becoming monotonous, and figurative choices usually feel apt to the world.
