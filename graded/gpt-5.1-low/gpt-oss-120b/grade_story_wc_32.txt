<question>4</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>The dominant stake—revive the crozier to break his “crystal cage” of destiny—is clear early and remains coherent. Internal conflict is not strongly opposed to his goal; his hopelessness doesn’t materially obstruct his actions. The result feels gently cumulative rather than tense, limiting the impact of the climax.</comment>

More reliance on implication and less explicit philosophizing would deepen the after-echo.</comment>
