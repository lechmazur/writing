<question>4</question><grade>6.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—reaching and understanding the place “where explanations fail”—remains mostly abstract and faces little overt resistance. As a result, escalation feels like a sequence of wonders rather than mounting pressure, and no option is visibly foreclosed. The quiet, lyric mode is fine, but a single sharp moment of risk, doubt, or potential loss would greatly deepen the stakes.</comment>

The resonance is real and coherent, but the authorial paraphrase keeps it out of the very top thematic band.</comment>
