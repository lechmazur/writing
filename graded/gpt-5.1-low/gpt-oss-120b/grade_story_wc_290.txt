<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stakes—securing the key and navigating severe claustrophobia—are clear early and interwoven. However, after he commits, the path offers no real reversal or complication; success comes with little resistance beyond reiterated internal fear. The result is a meaningful but relatively gentle escalation, more contemplative than harrowing.</comment>

Still, several thematic lines are stated outright (“not against stone, but against the forgetting of stories”; “not a weapon but a symbol”), tipping toward mild didacticism instead of fully trusting image and implication. This undercuts some potential lingering ambiguity and emotional afterglow.</comment>
