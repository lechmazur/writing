<question>4</question><grade>6.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—rescuing memory from oblivion—is clear but remains mostly conceptual, with limited on-page jeopardy or narrowing of options. The result is a beautifully sustained mood with only modest tension.</comment>

Still, the emotional aftertaste is earned through specifics rather than pure abstraction.</comment>

At times the register and metaphor frequency risk feeling more authorial-poetic than character-grounded, but not enough to break voice coherence.
