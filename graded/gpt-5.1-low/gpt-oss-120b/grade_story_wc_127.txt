A slightly tighter weave between setting and on-page jeopardy could push this higher.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—successfully capturing and bottling a counterfeit emotion for sale—is declared but never meaningfully threatened. The courier’s arrival promises future work rather than tightening current stakes. As a result, the piece reads as a beautifully rendered procedure more than a conflicted scene.</comment>

The thematic resonance is strong but would deepen if more conflict or consequence challenged Mara’s stance.</comment>
