<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether she can transmit guiding light without resorting to destructive fire—is present and does deepen as the sonar exposes “shadows of forgotten wars.” However, pressure remains mostly meditative; there’s no true narrowing of options or felt risk that her vow might fail or cost her something personal beyond inherited sorrow. The main escalation lies in the increasing reach and resonance of the sonar and the revelation that the gear’s infinity is a bridge, which is conceptually satisfying but low-tension. Still, the closing filament of light pulsing with sonar tones provides a strong, image-based thematic echo.</comment>
