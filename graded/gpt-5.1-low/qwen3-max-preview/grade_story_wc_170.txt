Environmental details mirror the thematic aim (fractured light as possibility) without blunt symbolism. The final image of her dissolving into “spring’s last sigh” is strongly charged by the previously established terrace atmosphere.</comment>

The escalation beat is the mirrors’ shiver and the third mirror reflecting her past, which deepens meaning but doesn’t significantly narrow options or demand a trade-off. Ambiguity feels purposeful and fertile rather than vague.</comment>
