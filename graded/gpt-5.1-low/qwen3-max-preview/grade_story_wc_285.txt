<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether and how the coachman will honor his palimpsest promise to “distill complexity into essence” in this specific passage across grief—is clear and coherently pressured by the fog, the passenger’s burden, and the ticking clock of the gutters.
