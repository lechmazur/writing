<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The central stake—reach him vs. lose herself—is established early and sharpened once she realizes she must weave thresholds instead of cloth and ultimately weave herself into the pattern. However, there’s little on-page resistance or reconsideration; once the method is discovered, the path to sacrifice is relatively untroubled and thus somewhat low on dynamic tension.
