<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether a dead terminal can be made to “breathe” again through engineering improvisation—is clear and coherent. As a result, escalation feels more like steady unfolding than pressured conflict with true trade-offs.</comment>

The theme is strong but edged toward sermonizing in the final paragraphs.</comment>
