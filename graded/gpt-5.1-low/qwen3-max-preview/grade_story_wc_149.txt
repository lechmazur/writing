<question>5</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>Themes of debt versus balance, love curdled into obligation, and the transformation of haunting into heritage emerge organically from recurring images (roots, runes, nectar, resonance) and the bride’s history. There is some tendency toward overload—figurative language in a high proportion of sentences and a few near-redundant intensifiers—which verges on purple at moments but does not materially obscure sense or closure. A rigorous line edit trimming a handful of stacked modifiers and reiterations would align it with top-tier literary venues.</comment>
