<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake is internal—how she will relate to inherited grief and encrypted joy—and it is signaled early via her “summoned” mission. Her choices are mostly how gently and reverently to proceed, which keeps tension low. <question>5</question><grade>7.1</grade><comment>Themes of joy within sorrow, grief as enduring shape, and resilience-as-lantern are coherent and reinforced by recurring images (frost, light, ascent, cracked lenses). This explicitness blunts subtext and keeps the thematic work from feeling fully emergent.</comment>

At times the narration edges into authorial essay-tone in its philosophical sentences, but not enough to break POV integrity.</comment>
