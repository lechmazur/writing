The decisive tightening comes with the realization that the heart must be a duet with the melody, transforming his approach from force to listening. Still, the conflict remains largely internal and contemplative; there’s little on-page threat of true failure beyond implied emotional cost, which keeps tension solid rather than bracing.</comment>

That overtness slightly dulls what is otherwise strong thematic resonance.</comment>
