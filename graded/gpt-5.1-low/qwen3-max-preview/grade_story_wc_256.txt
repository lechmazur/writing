Environmental detail is selective and thematically charged, with silence treated as layered matter rather than emptiness. The final image of the cracked, glowing lens clipped to her belt is strongly grounded in the physical space and carries the story’s charge.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The central conflict is internal and conceptual: reclaiming the texture of connection in a saturated, post-grid quiet. Pressure escalates gently from observation to experiment (striking the fork) to full participation (her sustained vowel that activates the lab), culminating in the release of trapped harmonies. The cost is subtle—she is emotionally undone and must relinquish comforting abstractions about silence and technology—but there is little sense of danger or foreclosed options. Effective for a lyric piece, though not operating at a high-tension level.</comment>
