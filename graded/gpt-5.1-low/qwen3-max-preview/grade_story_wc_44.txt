<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—understanding and reconciling what happened to shadows—remains consistent and escalates from observation to dangerous crossing to custodianship. Still, conflict stays primarily intellectual and metaphysical; there’s no strong counterforce, and the cost of failure or success is more implied than dramatized.
