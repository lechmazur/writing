While the setting doesn’t strongly constrain tactics in a practical sense, it functions as a macro-body the character is in dialogue with. The gear’s rust sloughs off and harmony emerges without cost beyond abstract doubt. As a result, escalation feels more like a graceful swell than genuine pressure.</comment>
