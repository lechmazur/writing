<question>4</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether she can use this ritual to conduct and preserve memory without breaking herself—is clear and threads the piece. The “before exhaustion hits” clock and her choice to push past it provide a meaningful tightening, but the concrete cost of that transgression remains mostly gestured at rather than dramatized in her body or options afterward. The tension is real, but the consequences could be more sharply felt to deepen impact.</comment>
