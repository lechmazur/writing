<question>7</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The line-level craft is generally strong: many sentences do triple duty for mood, worldbuilding, and theme; rhythm is varied; and images are often striking (“a dream carved in alabaster,” “names he’d carried like stones in his pockets”). It’s memorably inflected, but not quite revelatory.</comment>
