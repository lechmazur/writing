The drive lens is primarily accumulative/textural, and the piece honors that without lapses in orientation or logic.</comment>

Environment clearly constrains choices and crystallizes the closure, justifying a high score.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.3</grade><comment>The dominant stakes are existential and thematic—how Maren understands her work and place in time—rather than survival-based, and the main tightening comes when she recognizes the slide as part of a timewoven network, which forces a choice: keep it as artifact or enter the relay by leaving it. As a result, escalation is present but muted, more contemplative deepening than sharply felt tension.</comment>

However, a few lines verge on essayistic thesis (“simplicity was not the absence of complexity…”) and slightly blunt the subtextual power the images already carry.</comment>
