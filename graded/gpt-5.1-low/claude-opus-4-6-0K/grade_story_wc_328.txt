Recurring objects-as-setting (arrowhead, cordless phone) integrate with the environment to shape what’s possible. A bit more tactile interaction with the space at the peak (beyond “boots left the ground”) could further heighten its constraining role.</comment>

The price of staying (foregoing transcendence and vindication) is conceptually strong, if rendered more in idea than in visceral discomfort. The absence of a sharper external threat keeps tension slightly below peak-elite levels but still solid.</comment>
