The setting charges the final image without ever becoming a static postcard.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Maren can find a non-destructive way to bear and eventually express her apology—is clear from early on. The conflict remains primarily internal and textural, with no sharp reversal, but there is a meaningful tightening when she chooses to follow the lace’s spiral logic instead of her previous, harsher instinct. The cost of that choice—accepting a slow, uncertain path toward reconciliation—is felt but understated.</comment>
