<question>5</question><grade>8.6</grade><comment>Themes of grief, responsibility, and what “home” means in a multiverse context emerge organically from the particulars: sensory cartography as a way to know what instruments can’t, the shard as an inheritance, and the final decision to map a route to the father’s coordinates.
