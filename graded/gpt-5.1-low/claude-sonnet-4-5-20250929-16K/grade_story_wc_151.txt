<question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—protecting analog, off-grid identities for teens risking erasure from the network—is clear and compelling. However, there’s no genuine reversal or hard trade-off on the page; Maya’s tactics don’t have to adapt to new pressure and no live option is dramatically foreclosed in-scene.
