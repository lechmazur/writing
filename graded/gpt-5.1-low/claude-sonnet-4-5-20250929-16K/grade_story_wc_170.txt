The terrace’s acoustics and the prismatic light are not postcard garnish; they are the physical conditions her method depends on. The closing return to dusk, bells, and prism-lit mirrors anchors theme and mood without redundancy.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Aina can glimpse meaningful possibilities for Maya and herself without being destroyed by grief—is clear and signaled early. Her cost is the exhaustion and emotional exposure required to keep singing under a collapsing window, and the acceptance of possibility instead of certainty. The conflict remains mostly internal and lyrical, but within that mode it tightens once in a satisfying way.</comment>

Overall, the piece invites reflection on how art and imagination reframe loss without flattening it.</comment>
