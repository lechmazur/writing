<question>5</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The thematic throughline—care as the force that fractures and preserves time, attention as resistance to entropy—is tightly woven through concrete images (bees, beekeeper, invisible tending, the potter’s own careful work). The ending elegantly ties her craft, the oasis, and future acts of attention into one conceptual frame, making the piece linger. A touch more complexity or resistance (e.g., costs of such devotion) could push it higher.</comment>
