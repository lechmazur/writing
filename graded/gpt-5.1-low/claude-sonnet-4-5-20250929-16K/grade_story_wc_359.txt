<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—restoring the world’s capacity for wonder—is clear and scaled to the ritual. As a result, tension feels contemplative rather than escalating, limiting the category’s ceiling.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The thematic core—that wonder is essential, not a luxury, and can be reawakened through crafted silence—is organically bound to the premise and imagery (amber sheet, sculpted hush, chains breaking in reverse). The piece still leaves a resonant aftertaste, but with less ambiguity or complexity than top-tier work.</comment>
