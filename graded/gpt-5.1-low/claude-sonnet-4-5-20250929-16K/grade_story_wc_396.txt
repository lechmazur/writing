The world’s oddity (apple trees thriving in cold, vintage mirrors in a lighthouse) feels intentional and coherent within the lightly speculative frame. A slightly tighter link between environmental peril and immediate stakes could have pushed this higher.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether these clans will remain locked in feud or risk a new form of cooperation—is clear and anchored early in the dispute over fishing rights. Escalation is primarily internal/ethical rather than plot-driven, but the moment they ask, “Tell us how,” reflects a real shift under that pressure. The conflict remains quiet and assured rather than razor-edged, which slightly mutes urgency despite the strong conceptual stakes.</comment>
