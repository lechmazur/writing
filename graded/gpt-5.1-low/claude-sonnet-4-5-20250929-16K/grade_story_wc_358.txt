Conflict stays mostly conceptual, with limited felt risk or sacrifice in the moment.</comment>

Still, the ending reweights the premise in a resonant, concept-driven way.</comment>

It stops short of radical formal or emotional risk, but the synthesis is strong and specific.</comment>
