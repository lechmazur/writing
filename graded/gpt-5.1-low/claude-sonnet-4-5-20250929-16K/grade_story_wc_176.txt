<question>2</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The structure—a single scene in the sanctum interleaved with backstory—is clean and easy to follow, and causality under an accumulative lens is strong: fire → loss → guilt → discovery → project → first successful names. A touch more tactile interaction with the harshness of the cold could deepen the sense of constraint.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—preserving the founders’ names and thus transforming her guilt—is clear and consistently pursued, and her decision to devote years to this project is a credible form of Earned Stasis. The “miraculous” light alignment is a satisfying intensification but arrives without much prior friction testing her resolve. As a result, tension feels meditative rather than pressing, strong but not searing.</comment>

The perceptual signature—craft-focused, attentive to pattern and light—feels specific to Maren’s mind.
