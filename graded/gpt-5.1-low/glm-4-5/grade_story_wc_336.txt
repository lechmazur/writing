Strong textural setting work, but not yet using place as a decisive lever on action.</comment>

Internal and external conflict barely interweave; her desire and duty are aligned with no real friction. This makes the piece meditative rather than dramatically charged, limiting its viability at an elite venue.</comment>

Resonant in intention, somewhat platitudinous in execution.</comment>
