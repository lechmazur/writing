As a result, the emotional and narrative escalation feels gentle rather than truly tested.</comment>

This explicitness blunts what might otherwise be a more resonant, open-ended thematic afterglow.</comment>

Some turns of phrase drift toward generic spiritual vocabulary, but overall the syntax and rhythm feel deliberate rather than mechanical. The voice maintains coherence through the turn into the void and back, supporting the conceptual closure.</comment>

<question>8</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The “cosmic janitor” premise and the yellow lantern glass as a tool for empathic astronomy offer a fresh, appealing angle. Stronger grounding in unexpected specifics or a more idiosyncratic emotional logic could elevate originality further.</comment>
