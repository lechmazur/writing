The sanctuary-like grotto underpins the “radiant hush” tone and charges the final image of a suspended, protective space. It doesn’t quite constrain choices or force tactics, but it clearly shapes mood and concept.</comment>

There’s a mild textural escalation as the aurora intensifies and the memories cohere into a “pattern,” but no real narrowing of options or costly trade-off.
