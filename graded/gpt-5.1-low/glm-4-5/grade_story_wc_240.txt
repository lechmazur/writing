However, the environment rarely constrains tactics or forces specific choices; it’s more backdrop and metaphor than active pressure. The final brightening of the mushrooms does nicely charge the last image, lifting the score.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—redefining the nature of these “intruders” and the purpose of security—is clear but remains largely conceptual. <question>5</question><grade>5.9</grade><comment>Themes of boundaries, connection vs. protection, and “letting go” are coherent and well aligned with the imagery of permeable barriers and observation. The epiphany is more stated than earned through knotty, dramatized struggle. There is some resonance in the final image, but complexity is sacrificed for clarity.</comment>
