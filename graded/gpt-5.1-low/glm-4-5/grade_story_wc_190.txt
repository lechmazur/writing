As a result, the piece reads as a beautiful demonstration rather than a test of his commitment or method.</comment>

<question>8</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The core conceit—a gondola operator on a prism terrace who “teaches silence” as a healing medium, guided by a storm glass—is striking and distinctive. Where the piece falls short is in matching that conceptual inventiveness with equally inventive conflict or structural risk; the narrative arc itself is conventional and low-stakes. Still, the worldbuilding and central image are memorable and could anchor a high-level literary piece with further sharpening.</comment>
