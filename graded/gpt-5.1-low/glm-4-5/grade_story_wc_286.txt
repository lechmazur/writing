His “work” around the hives encounters no resistance and no moral or practical trade-off; the decision to return carries aspiration but no immediate price. This keeps tension gentle and meditative, effective for mood but modest as conflict.</comment>

The ideas are good, but they’re delivered too explicitly for top-tier literary subtlety.</comment>

Overall, the prose is polished and publication-ready with light tightening, but not razor-honed.</comment>
