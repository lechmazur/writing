Escalation is more a steady crescendo of description than a tightening of pressure or narrowing of options. However, diction leans toward generalized poeticism (“energy of ancient wisdom,” “warm blanket of serenity woven from starlight”) that could suit many similar fantasies, blurring a truly individual perceptual signature.
