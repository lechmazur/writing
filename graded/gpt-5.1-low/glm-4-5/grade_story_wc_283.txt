The repetition of “amber” and the warehouse can feel slightly incantatory without evolving much across the story.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.0</grade><comment>Stakes remain mostly conceptual: whether prophecy is fixed fate or shared possibility, with no real threat or consequence if Elara fails.
