<question>4</question><grade>6.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Elara will cling to predictive control or embrace co-creative, unpredictable magic—is present but under-pressured. Her “gasp in understanding” and quick acceptance dampen tension rather than sharpen it. As a result, the conflict reads more as an elegant revelation than a true test of values under strain.</comment>
