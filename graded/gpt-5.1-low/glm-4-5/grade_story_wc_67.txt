The environment feels particular to Elena’s world, though it mostly mirrors theme rather than actively constraining her choices.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—Elena’s need to capture fleeting emotion and, underneath that, to feel safe/anchored—is clear but remains largely abstract. The rocking chair’s stopping prompts a reflective shift, but it doesn’t arise from a hard choice or loss, so pressure never meaningfully tightens.</comment>

Overall momentum holds for a lyric piece, and no major figurative muddle or scaffolding undermines the closure image.</comment>
