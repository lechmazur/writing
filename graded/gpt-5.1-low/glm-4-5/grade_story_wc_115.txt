<question>7</question><grade>6.4</grade><comment>The prose is clean, grammatical, and easy to read, with a steady rhythm and few outright clunkers. Sentences often do at least two jobs (action plus mood or theme), which keeps momentum from stalling. <question>8</question><grade>6.7</grade><comment>The combination of an urban explorer, an abandoned industrial setting, and a late-night psychic call-in show is an appealing and somewhat fresh mash-up. However, the narrative ultimately travels a familiar arc toward the lesson that secrets must be confessed, without a non-obvious emotional or moral twist. The ingenuity lies more in premise ingredients than in the trajectory or payoff.</comment>
