Strong mood and specificity, but not fully leveraged as a tactical constraint or in the final image.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—loosening the town’s rigid mindset—is clear, and the synchronized displays create a conceptual escalation point.
