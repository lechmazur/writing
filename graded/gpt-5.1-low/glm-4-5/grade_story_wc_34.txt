<question>8</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The synthesis of a “ghost in code,” liminal storm-time courtyard, graffiti as communication medium, and an inherited flower-pressing plate as analog–digital bridge is genuinely inventive. Emotional stakes, however, remain relatively gentle and protected, so the originality functions more as elegant concept-building than as a vehicle for deep, surprising human trouble. Still, the story reads as a distinct vision rather than a template reskin.</comment>
