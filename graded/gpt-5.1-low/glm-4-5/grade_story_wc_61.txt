However, the voice lacks a strongly individualized perceptual signature for the sentimentalist; many lines could belong to any generic cosmic narrator. <question>8</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The premise—a non-destructive cosmic emissary bearing enlightenment, recording generational patterns via a gear—is a fresh-enough inflection on familiar “benevolent alien/godlike observer” tropes.
