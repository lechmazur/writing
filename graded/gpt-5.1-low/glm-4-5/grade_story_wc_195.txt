<question>4</question><grade>6.3</grade><comment>The dominant stake is Galen’s pursuit of forbidden/transformative knowledge, but the conflict is mostly internal and lightly contested: the text cautions him, yet he proceeds without any serious on-page hesitation or countervailing obligation. Tightening a few adjective-heavy constructions would sharpen impact, yet the line-level craft is already strong enough for competitive venues.</comment>
