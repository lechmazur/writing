The best integration is the glowing clipping/maze as a liminal object between physical space and dreamscape.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—restoring balance between dreams and reality—is signaled but remains very abstract. As a result, escalation feels more like a guided tour of steps than tightening pressure.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>5.6</grade><comment>The core theme—that dreams and reality should coexist rather than remain separate—is conceptually coherent with the covenant and intersection imagery. The ending veers toward moral summary, smoothing away complexity and cost. Subtext is thin; most philosophical content is stated plainly rather than left to resonate.</comment>

Without dialogue or sharply individual turns of phrase, the voice doesn’t yet reach distinctive-literary territory.</comment>
