Still, pressure is relatively soft: Maya never faces a sharp trade-off or a meaningful narrowing of options beyond working faster. The piece invites reflection about how we try to reach loved ones beyond our control, without spelling out a moral. At moments the sentiment teeters on the edge of the lyrical-sweet, but imagery and specificity mostly keep it grounded.</comment>
