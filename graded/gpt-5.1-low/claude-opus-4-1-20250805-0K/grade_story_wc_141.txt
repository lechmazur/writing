However, the text undercuts its own subtlety with explicit thesis statements like “The myth wasn’t about time travel or supernatural communication” and “truth sometimes lives in the spaces between fact and fiction,” which read as editorial gloss. Those on-the-nose lines reduce the room for the reader’s own inference.</comment>
