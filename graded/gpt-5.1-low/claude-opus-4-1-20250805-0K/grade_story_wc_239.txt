<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—resolving the universe’s expansion/contraction impasse by unraveling the illusion of opposition—is clear but remains largely abstract. The nominal ticking clock and loss of synchronization never meaningfully restrict Kaia’s options; she moves through the process without genuine resistance, doubt, or risk of failure. As a result, escalation feels more like a graceful unveiling than mounting pressure with visible cost.</comment>

The final reassembly of the blank dictionary with only “Continue” is an elegant, non-obvious payoff.
