The setting’s mood—washed, crystalline, liminal—perfectly matches the themes of return and absence. The final image of her in the rain-fractured harbor charges everything that came before.</comment>

Internal and external conflicts largely align; nothing seriously challenges her project or forces a painful choice. The result is thematically rich but low-tension, which keeps escalation from reaching the highest band.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>Themes of absence as grammar, communal memory, and transformation through fragmentation are thoughtfully and coherently woven through repeated images (blank pages, torn poem, valve, spray). However, the story sometimes states its thesis a bit baldly (“absence has its own grammar,” “compile fragments into meaning”), edging toward essayistic clarity rather than trusting implication. Even so, the emotional and conceptual aftereffect is strong and lingers.</comment>

Psychic distance stays steady in a close-ish third without gratuitous omniscience.
