Recurring environmental elements (mercury, stone, soil, thunder in the mountain’s bones) directly charge the final images. Subtext around chosen identity versus imposed role is present without didacticism.</comment>

There’s no dialogue to test, but internal language and imagery feel unified and character-true.</comment>
