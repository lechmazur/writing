<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>Conflict and stakes are intentionally low-key and mostly internal: whether and how she can perceive and honor hidden labor in a world that ignores it. However, she is never truly at risk of abandoning her task or worldview, so no option feels meaningfully foreclosed beyond the quiet choice of anonymity. The escalation is graceful but soft for the standard of an elite venue.</comment>
