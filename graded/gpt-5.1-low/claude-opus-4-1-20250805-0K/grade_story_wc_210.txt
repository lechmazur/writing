A slightly more functional interaction between landscape and his method could raise this toward the top band.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The core conflict—erase history for a clean slate vs. bear the burden of transforming it—is conceptually strong and legible. <question>5</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The theme of rejecting escapist wishes in favor of transformative struggle is coherent and well integrated with the premise of wish-magic and memory-essence. A touch more restraint and ambiguity in the last paragraph would give the piece greater literary subtlety.</comment>

Slightly sharper idiosyncrasy in phrasing or image choice could move this toward an elite-register voice.</comment>

Pushing further into the strangeness or consequences of this method could make the originality truly standout.</comment>
