<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake (protecting dream-cargo, especially the child’s nightmare) is clear and foregrounded before the pirate arrives. The conceptual stakes are high, but the on-page cost of victory is modest, keeping escalation strong but not fully bracing.</comment>
