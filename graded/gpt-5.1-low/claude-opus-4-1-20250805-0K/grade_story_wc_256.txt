There is no real counterforce on the page: institutional expectations, job security, or social censure never press back in-scene. The result is emotionally satisfying but short on felt cost or hard trade-offs.</comment>

Occasionally the voice edges toward essayistic abstraction, but it doesn’t break the POV frame.</comment>
