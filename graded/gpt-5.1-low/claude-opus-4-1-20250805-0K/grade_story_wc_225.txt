<question>5</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>Themes of fallible mentorship, the limits of knowledge, and the necessity—but cost—of self-forgiveness emerge organically from the chess and time-freeze metaphors, particularly in the mirroring of his mistake in the villagers’ final game.
