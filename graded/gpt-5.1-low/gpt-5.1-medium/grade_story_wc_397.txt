The main tightening is her decision to follow Runa’s cryptic instruction and cut the engine to listen, which forces a confrontation with her own premise. Cost is somewhat understated, but the relinquishing of the planned, administratively tidy death for renewed uncertainty reads as a genuine trade-off.</comment>

The final realization that the dominant hum is of things “choosing, moment by moment, not to end just yet” is a true conceptual repricing of everything before without tipping into moralizing. Ambiguity about what she’ll actually do next remains, but the story’s stance toward patience, disaster, and choice is satisfyingly complex.</comment>
