Liora’s agency at the end (tagging Tamar’s crest into the live data stream) is a meaningful, if quiet, act of resistance with a conceptual cost: she commits to a subversive career under a hostile system. The final reconfiguration—exile as a site of leverage rather than pure punishment—is resonant and conceptually rich.
