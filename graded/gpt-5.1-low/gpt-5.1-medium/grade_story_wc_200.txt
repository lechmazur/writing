<question>2</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The piece follows a clean descent-encounter-ascent structure, with each stage of the gorge deepening the core situation. Closure lands as a resonant reconfiguration rather than a sharp decisive act, with the world subtly “tilting” toward healing. The cost is somewhat soft (largely emotional and conceptual), but legible in her willingness to bear the dead’s confessions and reshape them.</comment>
<question>3</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The setting is vivid, specific, and inseparable from the story’s mechanics: the gorge’s acoustics and the cliff-harp literally channel the dead. Details like “a shy secret spilled across stone” and “a low throat of wind and water” establish both geography and mood in a way filtered through Mara’s sensibility. The gorge constrains the action (narrow spiral path, wind, embedded harp) and charges the final image once the sunrise fully reaches it. This is excellent atmospheric integration, just shy of transcendent because the environment could be pressed even harder against Mara’s body and risk.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The dominant stake—restoring balance so the gorge stops hoarding the village children’s joy—is clear and tight. Pressure coherently escalates as Mara descends and the harp’s “crooked chords” clarify into specific, risk-laden confessions. Her tactic of steering stories toward their absurd edge is a meaningful micro-choice that shifts the music from pure wail to rough harmony. However, real resistance or the possibility of failure is underplayed, so the conflict reads more as an assured ritual working correctly than a contested struggle with real peril.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.6</grade><comment>Themes of grief, superstition, and the salvational role of humor are woven through concrete choices rather than spelled out as thesis, especially in the line about grief fermenting into a “rough, saving humor.” Spectral anecdotes of dentures, fake hauntings, and self-made legends embody the idea that the dead’s shame and ridiculousness are part of healing memory. The final tilt toward healing, with giggles stitched into the music, offers a non-literal but clear thematic answer: integration over repression. A slightly sharper sense of cost or ambivalence at the end could raise the resonance further.</comment>

Dialogue snippets from the dead feel age- and personality-appropriate and carry subtext without crowding the lyric narration. <question>7</question><grade>8.7</grade><comment>The line-level work is dense, musical, and controlled, with most sentences doing multiple jobs (world-building, emotion, and theme at once). The crocodile-tooth backstory and the focus on children’s stolen laughter add distinctive, non-generic inflections. While the arc itself is simple, the conceptual synthesis and execution show high originality without feeling like a gimmick.</comment>
