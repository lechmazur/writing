Weather and environment actively shape action (Lio nearly turns back when it rains; puddles become the necessary map). <question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—resisting systemic erasure and indifference—is coherent, and pressure does tighten as Lio chooses to follow the impossible reflections and to speak into the archival conduit. Lio’s hesitation before depositing memory is the clearest on-page test of resolve, but the price of speaking (or of refusing) is only implied. The escalation is sufficient for quiet literary work, but an additional concrete pressure point could sharpen the cost.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>Theme—memory as resistance and indifference as the real tyrant—emerges organically from the erased booths, revised histories, and the markets in broken memories. However, the explicit sentence “the opposite of forgetting was not remembering, but refusing to look away” tips into stated thesis, slightly dulling the otherwise strong subtext. The ending still resonates, but with a hint of moralizing where image alone might have sufficed.</comment>
