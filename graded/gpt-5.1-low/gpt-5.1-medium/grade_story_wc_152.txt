<question>4</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The central stake—how to chart and survive a rebellion in a world where stories govern what can be remembered—is clearly signaled and meaningfully tightened once the prince asks for untraceable paths. Liora’s agency in offering an “absence” path is strong, but she faces little immediate personal jeopardy for helping him, so the conflict resolves more as an intellectual/moral tension than a fully felt gut-choice. Overall, resonance is high and lingers beyond the scene.</comment>
