<question>5</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>Theme—wonder as something shy and sideways, needing pauses and echoes to catch, versus a streamlined, forgetful city—is elegantly woven through objects (timer, mirrors), setting (scarred marble, wounded season), and his craft metaphors (“what a door remembered about being opened”).
