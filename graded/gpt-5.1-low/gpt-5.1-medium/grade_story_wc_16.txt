The binding of their own voice is a meaningful trade-off that deepens the conflict, even though no external opposition appears. The ending image of a “new music learning how to be a symphony of truth” reweights the entire piece without sermonizing. It’s rich and resonant, if a touch on-the-nose in its explicitness about “grammar of courage.”</comment>
