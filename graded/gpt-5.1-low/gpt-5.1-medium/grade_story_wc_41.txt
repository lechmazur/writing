<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether they can locate and recognize the “sound of growing older”—is clear and tightens as they move deeper into the fog, following and then converging on the driftwood markers. The final “price” is primarily the unsettling self-recognition, which is effective but quiet.</comment>

The story invites complex reflection on aging and identity without collapsing into a single thesis.</comment>
