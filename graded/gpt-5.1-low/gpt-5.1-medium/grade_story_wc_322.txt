Psychic distance stays stable; even the more abstract lines remain plausibly within this narrator’s lexicon under extreme emotion. Rhythm varies effectively between long, hallucinatory sentences and shorter, decisive beats around key choices.</comment>

The final non-choice—binding the doorway instead of crossing—offers a non-obvious but inevitable-feeling answer to the set-up question about pain and remembrance. The piece takes stylistic and conceptual risks that feel purposeful rather than gimmicky.</comment>
