However, the external peril resolves quickly once the commander speaks his son’s name, muting the sense of lasting cost or risk beyond that moment. The conflict is rich but could use one more explicit price or foreclosed option on the page.</comment>

Even so, the emotional resonance is strong and arises mostly from scene and image, not lecture.</comment>
