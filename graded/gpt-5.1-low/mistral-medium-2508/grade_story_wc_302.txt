The environment directly drives her sprint for the exit and charges the sense that this access to the unsaid is rare and costly. <question>4</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether she can capture and transform unsaid words before they’re erased by subsequent speech—is clear and sharpened when the watch shows the confidence man writing the second letter. Her ultimate decision to stop chasing the unsaid via magic and instead speak directly is a credible resolution that pays a price in relinquished power and mystery. The conflict remains mostly internal/lyric rather than sharply moral or interpersonal, but for this length and mode it feels sufficiently tense.</comment>

The resonance is strong but could be even more powerful with slightly less explicit statement of theme.</comment>
