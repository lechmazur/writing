<question>4</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Lena will re-engage with the dangerous, morally fraught work she burned for—is clear from early on. Her choice to pocket the key and set off constitutes an earned, costly turn that narrows her future. However, the final paragraph moves into slightly declarative moralizing (“Some debts weren’t paid in blood or codes, but…”) that spells out what the story has already shown. The thematic resonance is strong but would be more powerful with one less line of overt thesis.</comment>

Still, the core arc would survive with relatively modest changes to world specifics, which limits how far its originality reaches.
