<question>4</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The central stake—whether the brittle, “perfect” system of the library will hold or fall, and what Elias will choose when it doesn’t—is cued early and tightened by the forbidden act of printing the bottled whisper. Elias’s main act of agency is double-edged: using the whisper in an attempt to stabilize, and then accepting that the true, necessary outcome is collapse and awakening.
