<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Liora can stabilize or “anchor” reality/promise as friction vanishes—is present and escalates via the dust awakening, the aurora snapping into a ring, and the mirror-water showing her unraveling. Still, the opposition (“something…waiting for the friction to vanish”) remains faceless and untested, so the sense of struggle is more mythic inevitability than contested outcome.
