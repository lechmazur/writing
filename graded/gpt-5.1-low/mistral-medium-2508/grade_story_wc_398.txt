<question>4</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The dominant stake is Lior’s internal struggle to reinterpret a lost relationship and his choice to continue as sentinel without self-erasure. However, the conflict remains largely contemplative, with few real alternatives dramatized beyond stay/leave, so escalation feels more tonal than decisively narrowing. Complexity is maintained without collapsing into a neat moral.</comment>
