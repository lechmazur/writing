While the geography doesn’t tightly constrain options, it strongly charges the central images and final resonance.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stakes are internal (self-definition versus inherited violence and village suspicion) with a concrete external test in the injured boy. The cost is more conceptual and ongoing—living under watch, holding both destructive and healing fire—than immediate loss, which keeps escalation relatively quiet. Still, her renewed choice at the next alarm reads as an earned recommitment under pressure.</comment>
