<question>2</question><grade>6.0</grade><comment>The structure is linear and coherent: she approaches the lighthouse, performs the ritual, and descends after dawn, with clear spatial and temporal anchors. <question>3</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The emerald lighthouse, starry sky, and glass lantern create a strong, unified atmospheric frame that recurs through the ending image. Still, the environment almost never constrains or complicates her choices; it’s a beautiful stage rather than an active pressure. Greater use of weather, tower height, or isolation to shape action would raise this.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>3.8</grade><comment>Stakes are mostly declared (“role was more crucial than ever”) rather than dramatized through risk or opposition. The only “pressure” is the abstract responsibility to shape dawn, which is never concretely tested. As a result, tension remains flat and the narrative functions more as a mood piece than a conflict-driven story.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>5.5</grade><comment>Themes of tradition vs. digital ephemerality and light/shadow as a way of shaping reality are clear and coherent. The cyclical ending reinforces continuity but doesn’t significantly reweight what came before. The piece invites mild reflection but stops short of a complex or surprising thematic insight.</comment>
