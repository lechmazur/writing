<question>4</question><grade>5.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether she can navigate grief and reclaim some relation to her lost world—is present but stays largely abstract. As a result, tension never genuinely tightens, and the price she pays is conceptual rather than viscerally felt.</comment>
