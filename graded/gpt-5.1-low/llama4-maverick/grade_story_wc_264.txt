<question>4</question><grade>4.8</grade><comment>Stakes are declared—safeguard the archive, nurture dormant sparks in a devastated world—but they remain mostly abstract. Still, the treatment skews tidy and declarative (“a testament to the power of the divergent archive”) rather than opening up richer ambiguity or moral texture.
