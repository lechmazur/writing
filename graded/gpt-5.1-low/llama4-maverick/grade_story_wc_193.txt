<question>3</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>The bioluminescent mushroom farm is vividly evoked at the beginning and returns in the final image, giving the piece a coherent, luminous atmosphere. Still, the environment rarely constrains choices or tactics—the lab and farm could be swapped for many other settings without altering the plot. The mushrooms charge the closing tone but do not shape the conflict itself.</comment>

The final resonance is pleasant but not deeply earned or surprising.</comment>

Psychic distance stays steady but safe, rarely diving into idiosyncratic perception. There is no dialogue to complicate or enrich the vocal texture.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>5.8</grade><comment>Line-level craft is competent, with clear sentences and straightforward sequencing, making the story easy to parse.
