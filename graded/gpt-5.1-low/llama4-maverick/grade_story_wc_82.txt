<question>5</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>Themes of transmuting grief into beauty, the cost of luminous torment, and yearning for transcendence are coherently woven through lanterns, cranes, light, and the distant basilica. However, the piece leans on direct thematic formulations (“transforming sorrow into something radiant,” “luminous torment”) instead of letting subtext do more of the work, which blunts subtlety. Emotional aftereffect is gentle and genuine but not piercing.</comment>

However, the story doesn’t fully exploit the more original aspects (dream recording, detached shadow) beyond evocative mention, and the emotional trajectory hews to a well-trodden healing-through-art pattern.
