<question>7</question><grade>6.2</grade><comment>The prose is grammatically clean and flows smoothly, but it relies heavily on repetition and abstract phrasing (“as she worked,” “as the night wore on,” “even in the darkest moments”) that add little new information. Some images are fresh (cells dividing alongside her work), but figurative language is used sparingly and safely.
