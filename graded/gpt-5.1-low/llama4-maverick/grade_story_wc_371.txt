The final image does draw on this setting as a site of renewal, which gives it modest closure charge.</comment>

The lack of concrete past wrongdoing softens the moral or emotional complexity of “absolution.” The result is pleasant and mildly resonant but veers toward sentimentality and didactic closure.</comment>

<question>8</question><grade>5.2</grade><comment>The combination of a secret tea house, guerrilla gardener, and mysterious bread has potential, and “creeping normality noticer” hints at a fresh angle.
