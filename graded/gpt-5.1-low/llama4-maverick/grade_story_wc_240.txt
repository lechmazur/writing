The recurring farm imagery does charge the final departure, but mostly in mood, not in altered options.</comment>

<question>5</question><grade>5.0</grade><comment>Theme is explicit and consistent—interconnectedness, permeability of boundaries, and “letting go”—but it is often told in thesis-like phrases rather than emerging from action (“She believed that by doing so, she could revolutionize the way people interacted…”). Still, the narrative is clear and readable, with no major POV slips.</comment>

It shows glimmers of a distinctive vision but doesn’t fully risk or commit to a truly novel narrative or thematic move.</comment>
