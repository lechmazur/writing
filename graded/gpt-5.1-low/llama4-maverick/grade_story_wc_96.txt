<question>7</question><grade>6.4</grade><comment>The prose is clean and grammatically sound, and most sentences advance either mood or information without glaring clutter. The larger arc—art and community rebuilding a blighted city—is familiar and plays out in fairly expected beats without a truly non-obvious turn or cost.
