The overall effect is languid and somewhat static, sapping momentum in a story that already has limited plot.</comment>
<question>8</question><grade>6.3</grade><comment>The combination of a crumbling amphitheater inside a breathing dormant volcano, a floodgate machinist, and decryption via envelope adhesive is intriguingly specific and not a stock fantasy setup.
