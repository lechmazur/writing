The ampoule and garden are charged symbolically, but more through exposition than through tactile interaction.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>3.8</grade><comment>Stakes remain almost entirely abstract: understanding the “secret of the garden’s downfall” and welcoming nuance into certainty. As a result, pressure barely escalates and conflict never crystallizes beyond a wistful tone.</comment>
