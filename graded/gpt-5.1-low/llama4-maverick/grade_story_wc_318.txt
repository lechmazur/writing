Repetitions and stock phrasing flatten what could be a more idiosyncratic, character-colored narration. <question>8</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The central conceit—a leaf blower symphony conductor in an echo tunnel resurrecting a household’s laughter—is genuinely fresh and memorable.
