The register of phrases like “luminous regret” and “metamorphose her understanding” sometimes feels more authorial than character-rooted, slightly flattening individuality. <question>8</question><grade>6.2</grade><comment>The combination of a dream-sharing world, a blooming sky library, and a “dusk noticer” studying decades of silent footage is appealing and somewhat fresh. The piece takes stylistic risks with its lush lyricism and conceptual frame, but it doesn’t push those risks into surprising character or plot turns.
