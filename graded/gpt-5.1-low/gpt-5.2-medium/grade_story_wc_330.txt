<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake is Rowan’s internal, craft-and-silence relationship, with an external corollary in reviving the wounded carousel horse and, by extension, the dead circus train. The final image—train stirring, mended horse led forward, note carried rather than smothered—offers a resonant, non-literal answer to the opening problem. The piece edges close to articulating its insight overtly, but stays just inside the boundary of felt rather than preached.</comment>

Occasionally the lushness risks slight over-saturation, but it never confuses POV or temporal stance.</comment>

No significant loops or scaffolding artifacts slow the crucial beats of discovery, repair, and song.</comment>
