<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—reclaiming the city’s light without breaking the Covenant—is clear early and coherently pursued. The resulting conflict feels meaningful but somewhat low in on-page cost and tension.</comment>

Still, the integration of craft, truth, and restoration is resonant and specific.</comment>

It falls just short of the rarefied 9+ band because the emotional and moral turns, while elegant, remain relatively safe and un-surprising.</comment>
