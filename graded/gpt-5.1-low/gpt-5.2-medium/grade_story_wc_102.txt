<question>4</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether she can transform paralyzing, stored love into a living current—is clear early and tightens through the breathing ritual and the demand to “heal it the way she would heal a cracked negative.” Escalation is primarily internal but legible: from abstract ache → visualized embers/face → direct confrontation with the knot → imaginative re-do of the missed blessing. The cost is visible in her surrender of pride and willingness to re-enter painful memory, and in the relinquishing of her image-hoarding artistic stance.
