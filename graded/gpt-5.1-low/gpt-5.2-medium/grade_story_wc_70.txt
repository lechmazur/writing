The final images of lit street lamps and revived drones directly arise from and recharge these environmental details.</comment>

The closing line does flirt with aphorism, tipping a bit toward a stated moral rather than leaving it wholly in image and implication. Still, the piece largely trusts scenes and metaphors to carry its thematic weight.</comment>
