It’s hard to imagine the story transplanted from this specific physical and cosmic environment without losing most of its power.</comment>

Her final choice to run the halo fragment alone and offload the borrowed selves forecloses a path and carries a cost in identity and stored emotion. The blank broadside aligning townspeople’s sorrows is a strong, subtext-laden image that lingers. The final sentence comes close to stating the thesis outright, but it’s earned by the preceding concrete work rather than feeling like a bolt-on moral.</comment>
