The setting constrains the entire method of resistance (photographic plates, timed exposures) and charges the final image of carrying “measured light” back into the city. World rules feel internally consistent and learned through action, not exposition.</comment>

Agency is internal but decisive: the choice to adopt Lysa’s method and recast poems as timed scars forecloses the more obvious path of louder defiance. Conflict is primarily interior and could bear one more concrete external friction point, but it still reads as consequential.</comment>
