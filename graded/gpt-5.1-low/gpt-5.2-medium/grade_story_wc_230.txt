Her climactic choice to mislead the captain and bury the real eye forecloses the path of unmitigated truth with a clear ethical and personal cost. The tension is more moral and psychological than kinetic, but it tightens convincingly under the situational/causal lens.</comment>

The final image of burying the eye beneath the truffle bed reframes secrecy as a kind of cultivated grace while still acknowledging its fracture and cost. The piece resists easy moralizing and leaves room for unease about the compromises being made.</comment>
