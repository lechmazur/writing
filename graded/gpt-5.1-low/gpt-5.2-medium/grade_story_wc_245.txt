<question>5</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>Themes of grief, navigation, and inner vs. outer maps are woven tightly through concrete motifs (maps burned into vellum, the ring as an “eye,” the stillpoint, language-as-touch), and the final map “with no borders, only currents” elegantly reweights everything.
