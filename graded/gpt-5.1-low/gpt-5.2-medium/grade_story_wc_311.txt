<question>4</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>The stakes are primarily internal—whether he can metabolize the “echo of sacrifice” or be hollowed out by it—and they’re clearly articulated through his need to name fears. His agency is quiet but real: choosing to transform each voice into an object on the map and finally deciding to hide the last crystal under blank ocean, then to walk away bearing “distant intimacies,” forecloses a path of numb collection. The final move—hiding the tiny sob-crystal in the blank ocean so future keepers can “speak to it kindly”—reprices the entire map and vocation in terms of compassion over control.
