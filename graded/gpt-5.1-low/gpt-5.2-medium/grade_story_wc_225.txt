The structure balances puzzle and interiority without losing drive. Slightly more on-page resistance or complication in solving the maze could sharpen the sense of jeopardy.</comment>

The environment evolves with events (hush tightening, labyrinth dimming) and directly shapes what is possible. World rules are consistent and learned through action rather than exposition.</comment>

The final gate and mirrored self-image crystallize the conflict into a concrete decision (“return” versus “conquer”). Escalation is more a steady deepening than sharp reversal, leaving tension strong but not extreme.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>Themes of responsibility, the morality of strategy, and self-forgiveness are woven through chess, tapestry, and labyrinth imagery in ways that mostly avoid overt moralizing. The quiet, non-triumphal release of the town underscores a move away from heroic ego. The narrative modulates well from atmospheric description to more pressurized interior beats as she enters the labyrinth. There is no head-hopping or register drift that breaks the illusion.</comment>
