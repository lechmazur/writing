The ending resonates, but the moral is spelled out more than it trusts the images to carry it.</comment>

<question>8</question><grade>8.3</grade><comment>The blend of SF apparatus (calculating gear, bioluminescent tech) with mystical, intergenerational ritual in a “nebula garden” feels fresh and specific. The structural arc—a quiet visitation and conceptual reframing rather than a dramatic confrontation—is a tasteful risk that works. Slightly less explicit moralizing and a sharper on-page cost would lift this into a more singularly striking tier.</comment>
