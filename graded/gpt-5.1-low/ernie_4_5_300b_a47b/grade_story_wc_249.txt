The choice to drum is a real act of agency that resolves the internal tension and shifts the lagoon’s response, yet no path is visibly foreclosed beyond his prior isolation. As a result, tension is present and coherent, but not especially sharp.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>Themes of shared memory, transforming trauma into connection, and choosing to “breathe life into a shared dream” emerge organically from the sound-based setting and Eli’s history as a guinea pig. The emotional aftereffect is gentle and sincere, but slightly sentimental and on-the-nose for top-tier literary venues.</comment>
