<question>3</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The subway utility tunnel and underground booth are vividly evoked—“concrete veins,” damp earth, old copper, flickering bulbs—and they meaningfully mirror the story’s concern with hidden, infrastructural connections. <question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake is emotional and thematic: whether Mara can transform misdirected longing into genuine connection. However, Mara never faces a sharp dilemma or visible sacrifice; she’s competent and unshaken, so tension stays gentle rather than tightening into a hard trade-off.
