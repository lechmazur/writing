The music box and pin function as a kind of micro-setting around which action coheres, but the sanctuary itself could be swapped for another quiet room with modest rewrites.</comment>

The “risk” of cellular memory is asserted but never cashed out in cost, and there’s little sense that failure would truly endanger Lirael or anyone else.
