<question>5</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>Themes of systems turned into prisons, subversion from within, and “words as lockpicks” are coherently threaded through Maren’s job, the sundial, and the mentor’s maxim. Moral complexity is low; the revolution is framed as unambiguously right with little nuance about potential costs or collateral.</comment>
