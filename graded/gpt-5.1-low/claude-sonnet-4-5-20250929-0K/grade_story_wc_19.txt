World rules feel consistent and are learned via action rather than dry exposition. Some atmospheric flourishes risk over-ornamentation, but they mostly deepen rather than blur the setting.</comment>

The final decision to “choose the sky” over bunkers is an earned stance crystallization with a visible conceptual and relational cost.
