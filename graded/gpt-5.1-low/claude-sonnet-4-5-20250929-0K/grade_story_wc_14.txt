The environment mainly charges meaning rather than constraining physical choice, but the transition from distorted groves to the proportionate oak at the center powerfully underwrites the final realization.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake is interior and conceptual—can she meaningfully “record” in a world where ordinary history has gone silent, and what is the worth of beautiful untruths? However, the conflict is tightly leashed and largely unopposed; there’s little explicit cost or resistance on the page beyond the abstract loss implied by the town’s homogenized time and her quiet decision to keep falsifying.</comment>
