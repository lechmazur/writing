<question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Cosima can make her salvaging of sounds matter to others and thus reclaim a sense of purpose—is legible and resolved. The emotional cost is mostly in what she has already lost (home, status) and in giving the box away, rather than in on-page jeopardy or difficult trade-offs. As a result, escalation feels gentle rather than pressing, effective but not gripping.</comment>
