The crystals function as more than décor by responding to her confession, though the environment constrains mood more than concrete options. The final image of her walking back “toward the hidden sunrise” ties tone and place together effectively.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The core conflict—fear of failing as an inventor versus the pain of never trying—is clearly articulated and returns in the key beats with her sketchbook and the crystals’ response. However, opposition remains almost entirely internal and is resolved without real friction from countervailing desires once the sanctuary “answers” her. The piece could benefit from one sharper moment where continuing as a scribe feels genuinely tempting or safer to heighten the trade-off.</comment>
