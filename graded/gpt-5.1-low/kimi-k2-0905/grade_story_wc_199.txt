Consequences are almost entirely positive, with minimal felt trade-off when memories and monuments lift away. The conflict therefore functions more as an elegant puzzle and ritual than as a truly tested dilemma.</comment>

The emotional aftereffect is tender and contemplative rather than piercing, which suits the fable mode but limits depth.</comment>
