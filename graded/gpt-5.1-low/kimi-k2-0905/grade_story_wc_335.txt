<question>4</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The central stakes—Lira’s choice between self-sacrificial return that saves the library versus enduring exile to preserve memory and mentor another—are clear, and the tea-leaf reading usefully tightens pressure by framing a specific, costly alternative.
