<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—securing and embodying wonder before the Council can outlaw it—is intriguing, and there is a discernible tightening as Elian moves from rumor to contact with the Vein to stepping into the sculpted hush and finally swallowing the sheet.
