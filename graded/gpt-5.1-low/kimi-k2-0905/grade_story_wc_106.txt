<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether the thief will damage the world further to satisfy a client’s desperate wish—is signaled well and tightened when the prior erasure is revealed via the ring inscription. Escalation is thus more intellectual than visceral, effective but shy of maximal intensity for this premise.</comment>

The closing injunction in the flowers (“go home, memory is enough”) lands as earned, bittersweet wisdom rather than platitude.</comment>
