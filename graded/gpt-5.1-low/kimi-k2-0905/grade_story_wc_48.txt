<question>5</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>Themes of artistic responsibility, the afterlife of abandoned stories, and the nature of hope emerge organically from the apparitions and Mara’s choices, not just from stated ideas. <question>7</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>Line-level craft is strong: sentences vary in rhythm and length, images are often doing double duty for setting and psychology, and there’s minimal redundancy or scaffolding. <question>8</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The core premise—unfinished fictional elements manifesting as ephemeral hopes in a liminal tidal nexus—is strikingly original and thoughtfully explored.
