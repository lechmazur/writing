<question>5</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>Themes of time, control, and “befriending uncertainty in planning” are strongly woven through recurring images (broken clocks, the handless mechanism, the compass without north, the waterfall of time) and culminate in a clear conceptual repricing of certainty itself. The piece still invites reflection, but some wisdom is told rather than entirely earned through subtext.</comment>
