<question>5</question><grade>9.1</grade><comment>Thematic throughline—joy smuggled into sorrow, delight as resistance within oppressive structures—is woven through concrete images (laughing-mouth water stains spiraling into music, the captain tasting “wind’s metal” while knowing the tree waits below, the seed taped into the diary) rather than abstract statements.
