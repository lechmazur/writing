<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—discovering the texture of connection—is clearly signaled and followed, and pressure accumulates via the gallery awakening, the thread heating/glowing, and the revelation that the gallery is a neural network. The main tightening is the gallery speaking through the composite man and the subsequent realization, which works conceptually but never feels perilous or costly on the page. The result is a strong but low-tension escalation suited to lyric mode rather than a fully pressurized crucible.</comment>

The final conceptual repricing is resonant but slightly over-articulated, blunting some lingering ambiguity. Still, the thematic spine is integrated and meaningful, not tacked on.</comment>
