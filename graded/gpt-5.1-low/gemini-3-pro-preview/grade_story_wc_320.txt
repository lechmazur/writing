<question>2</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The story tracks cleanly as a single causal sequence: climb → set up relic → interference overwhelms signal → new tactic → successful capture → tidy exit. The pressure is real but stays at an intellectual/technical level; her body and safety never feel truly in jeopardy, which flattens potential stakes.</comment>

However, this is stated somewhat directly rather than fully earned via subtext and on-page conflict. The piece invites reflection but edges toward explicit paraphrase of its own theme in a couple of lines.</comment>
