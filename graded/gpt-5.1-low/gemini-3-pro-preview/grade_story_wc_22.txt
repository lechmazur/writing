<question>2</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The causal chain is clean: coachman arrives, reads the birds, speaks the dead language, casts seeds, receives concord, and departs, all in a single, coherent scene. Closure lands via a resonant reconfiguration: the battlefield-ocean is literally and symbolically reweighted as the garden takes hold. The main structural weakness is the absence of any complication or setback, which flattens the arc into a graceful curve rather than a true tightening.</comment>

Sensory detail is varied (smell of old rain and iron, sound of birds, texture of mossy railings) and filtered through Silas’s workmanlike perspective. While the jetty doesn’t constrain his choices in a tactical sense, it is inseparable from the piece’s mood and meaning.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—reviving a dead battlefield-ocean through seeds and language—is clear, but the conflict is almost entirely one-sided and ritualized. The pressure does not really escalate; Silas performs his usual act, and concord is granted without friction or delay. There is no live possibility of failure on the page, nor any sacrifice demanded of him in this instance beyond routine effort. As a result, tension and on-page cost remain modest despite the large-scale thematic stakes.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>Themes of regeneration after war, reconciliation between elements (“submerged concord”), and memory as a kind of fertilizer emerge organically from images rather than explicit moralizing. Some phrasing edges toward overt symbolism (“stitching the wound of the world closed”) but generally stays grounded in scene. The final sense of “inevitable victory of the roots” offers a resonant, non-literal answer about time, damage, and repair without collapsing into sermon.</comment>

Psychic distance remains stable—close enough to share Silas’s specialized perceptions without head-hopping. There is very little dialogue, but the narrative voice itself carries mood and information effectively.</comment>

Line-level craft is strong enough for publication with only light trimming for concision.</comment>

Still, the vision coheres as something memorable rather than a recombined template.</comment>
