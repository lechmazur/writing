<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—expose the Emperor’s fraud and topple a three-century dynasty—is clearly signaled and tightly linked to Elian’s immediate task. <question>5</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The piece coherently explores the fragility of power built on spectacle and myth, and the idea that reality can be literally “contained” and then released through careful, subversive attention.
