The storm-as-flesh imagery and the porous blur between inside and outside evolve with the ritual and ship’s reappearance, charging the final image. <question>4</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The dominant stake—reversing Silas’s exile and proving his courage—is clear and present from early on and tightens as Elara initiates the machine. However, opposition remains mostly atmospheric; there are no real reversals, tactical adaptations, or visible prices beyond fear and risk-taking. The climax succeeds but tilts toward inevitability rather than a hard-won, precarious victory.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>Themes of memory, mapping, and the thin border between “lost and found” emerge organically from Silas’s ciphers, the Weeping, and Elara’s role rather than through overt moralizing. There is no head-hopping or stance slippage; psychic distance is steady and modulates subtly as tension rises.
