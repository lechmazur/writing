<question>2</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The structure is clean: Elian enters, senses a focal wound, investigates, understands, and leaves, with the tapestry/diary reveal anchoring closure. The piece could reach higher if Elian’s realization tangibly altered his options or stance beyond quiet completion.</comment>

The environment meaningfully charges the imagery at the end, with the silo left on “eternal, dreamless vigil.” It rarely constrains concrete choices, though, functioning more as atmosphere than as a tactical obstacle.</comment>
