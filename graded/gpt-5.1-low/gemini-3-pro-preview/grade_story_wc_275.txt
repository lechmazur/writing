Some lines verge on thesis statements, edging toward explicitness rather than pure subtext. Nonetheless, the final image reweights the premise in a resonant, non-literal way.</comment>

<question>8</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>The synthesis of post-apocalyptic glass-dome agriculture with the forbidden practice of inculcating plants with moral traits feels fresh and conceptually ambitious. The “engraved quiet” and spores-of-mercy roses give the premise a distinctive texture and closure image that would be hard to replicate elsewhere. While the broad themes of pacifism vs violence are familiar, they’re instantiated in a notably inventive way.</comment>
