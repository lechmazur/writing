<question>4</question><grade>7.9</grade><comment>The dominant stake—preserving her mind and life from the invasive noise—is clear and tied to the encroaching beings, with the “screaming static” escalating as they approach. <question>5</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>Themes of engineered peace, the weight of silence, and the transformation of knowledge (mapping resonance into weaponized quiet) emerge organically from particulars: ice as script, the echo stone, the sonic fabric of reality. Some thematic notes are stated rather directly (“a state of hammered repose…earned through endurance”), which slightly blunts their subtextual power.</comment>
