<question>4</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>The dominant stakes—physical survival under embargo and preservation of social fabric—are clearly articulated, and the need for secrecy adds a secondary pressure. As a result, the piece reads as strong setup plus affirmation rather than a fully tested crisis.</comment>

Still, the integration of ritual, object, and strategy into a coherent moral stance is strong.</comment>
