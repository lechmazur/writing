The environment, however, mostly supports atmosphere rather than constraining what he can do; the story would largely function in a different quiet room, though the open sky and fireworks do charge the final image. Overall, specificity is strong even if functional pressure from setting is modest.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The central conflict—opening the clasp without “violence” and in harmony with its emotional resonance—is conceptually interesting and clearly stated. That lack of real jeopardy or trade-off keeps tension from fully sharpening.</comment>

The final image—fireworks exploding as the clasp “sighs” and he names the silence—is an original synthesis of inner and outer spectacle.
