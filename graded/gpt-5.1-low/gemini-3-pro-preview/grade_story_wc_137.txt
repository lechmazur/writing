<question>2</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The structure is clean: arrival with a practical archiving task, encounter with the tile and maps, entangled memories as the turn, and a reoriented departure that closes the emotional and conceptual arc. The environment mostly charges image and mood rather than constraining physical choice, which keeps it just shy of the very highest band.</comment>

At times the piece edges into overt thesis statements (“mathematical proof that the chaos of human sorrow could be ordered into a peaceful sum”), which slightly undercuts the otherwise organic emergence of meaning.</comment>

Occasional abstract formulations (“cognitive resonance,” “emotional archive”) feel a bit written-about rather than directly perceived, but overall the perceptual signature is strong and distinct.</comment>
