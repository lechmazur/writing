<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—stabilizing the madness of an accelerated world by locating the intersection of dreams—is clearly signaled and fulfilled via the maze-vision. The conflict works, but it could use at least one sharper, on-page trade-off or near-failure to feel fully tested.</comment>
