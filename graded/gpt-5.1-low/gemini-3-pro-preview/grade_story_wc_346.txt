<question>5</question><grade>8.6</grade><comment>The thematic throughline—valuing small, tender affect (“being held safely in the dark”) over monumental histories at the end of everything—is strong and embodied in the puppet’s flawed surface and the quiet, anti-grandiose nature of the chosen legacy.
