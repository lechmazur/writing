<question>5</question><grade>8.3</grade><comment>Themes of memory versus oblivion, the persistence of connection, and the cost of recommencing after erasure are carried through concrete motifs: puzzle edges as boundaries, the amber bead as fossilized joy, and the loom as a machine for defying entropy.
