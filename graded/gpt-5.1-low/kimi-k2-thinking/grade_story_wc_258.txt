<question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The primary stake—whether the architect can reframe his fallen faith in single chains into a more resilient, multi-path vision of the future—is clear but remains mostly conceptual. The ending still resonates, but a touch less explicit moralizing would heighten its literary subtlety.</comment>
