<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The primary stake—Mira’s entrapment in an emotional and temporal loop versus her emerging need to connect and free both herself and the callers—is legible and escalates. However, the conflict remains largely internal and atmospherically textured; the sense of danger or concrete risk beyond malaise and loneliness is muted. The price of breaking the loop (ending the calls, physical pain, stepping into an unknown life) is present but could be made more viscerally fraught.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The thematic thread of endings as beginnings is tightly woven through the loop, the calls, and Mira’s departure from the tunnel. The repeated moral-style formulations make the ending feel didactic rather than letting the images (shattered disc, silent phone, her walk into night) carry the resonance. There is emotional honesty, but little ambiguity or resistance to the central idea.</comment>

Nonetheless, there are no significant artifacts, and the closure image is clear and legible.</comment>
