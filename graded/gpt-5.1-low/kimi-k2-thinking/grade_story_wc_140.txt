<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether the survivor can assemble a felt sense of identity from erased records and borrowed stories—is clear and consistently pressured by his liminal status in the Grid. The final act of putting the weighted box within reach of the tide crystallizes his stance rather than resolving the systemic threat, which works for an accumulative lyric piece.
