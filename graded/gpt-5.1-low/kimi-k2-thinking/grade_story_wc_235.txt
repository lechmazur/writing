<question>5</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>Themes of time as non-linear, selfhood as narrative, and the ambivalent seduction of nostalgia are deeply woven through images (shattered mirror, impossible dates, multiplying past selves) and resolved in the idea that “the chronicler dies but the dreaming continues.” The final beats offer a resonant answer—embracing being story instead of stable self—without resorting to a neat moral, and the grave/scroll motifs are reweighted at the end.
