The jetty and reef directly charge the final image, where sea, sky, and recovering land unify under the “narrow vastness.” This environmental web constrains and enables choices, elevating the atmosphere well beyond ornament.</comment>

There’s no concrete loss, sacrifice, or meaningful foreclosed path; the soil yields to his methods on the first serious attempt we see. As a result, the piece is moving but low-tension, relying on mood rather than escalating conflict.</comment>

The piece retains complexity—this is not tidy moralizing—but trims could heighten subtlety.</comment>
