<question>2</question><grade>8.7</grade><comment>The structure is clean: descent, investigation, conceptual turn, and ascent, all under a single continuous lens. The closure is a resonant reconfiguration as the snow globe shifts from “singularity of memory” to artifact; Cassian’s stance toward grief changes with conceptual cost. Nothing important is left hanging, and the ending’s logic is fully supported by prior beats.</comment>
<question>3</question><grade>9.0</grade><comment>The sunken library is vivid, specific, and thematically charged—“colonized by anemones and sorrow” is both image and thesis. Environment and recurring object fuse: the brass chest, the drowned books, and the pressing water concretize the idea of preserved-yet-decaying memory and constrain the action to delicate, slow work. The setting meaningfully charges the final image of leaving the library to sediment while carrying the globe to the surface. Details are economical yet immersive, and they always serve character and theme.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—how to relate to loss without being trapped by it—is clear and coherent, with Mort’s line (“melancholy doesn’t care about equations”) sharpening the pressure. Escalation is quiet but present: Cassian moves from measurement to insight to a redefinition of letting go, culminating in the choice to treat the globe as artifact rather than prison. The cost is conceptual rather than dramatic, but it is legible: he surrenders the illusion that understanding will fully neutralize grief. The piece could edge higher with one more concrete, felt test of his resolve under pressure.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>Themes of grief, memory, and the persistence of messages are woven tightly through objects and setting; “The message persists. However, some lines become overt thesis statements (“Letting go occurs when the object of loss becomes the subject of study”), tipping into aphoristic telling rather than leaving more work for subtext. This slight didactic edge keeps the piece just below elite-tier thematic subtlety.</comment>

Psychic distance stays stable; any semi-omniscient flourishes are grounded in Mort’s nature and the story’s conceit. Rhythm and syntax vary enough to sustain musicality without lapsing into ornament for its own sake.</comment>

Momentum holds throughout despite the reflective mode.</comment>
