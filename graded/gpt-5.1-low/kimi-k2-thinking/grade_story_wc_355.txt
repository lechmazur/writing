Still, the final image of the quiet workshop under the indifferent stars is well-charged by prior details.</comment>

<question>8</question><grade>8.3</grade><comment>The fusion of radio frequencies, varieties of silence, and lockpicking a diary via resonance is a fresh, memorable conceit. It’s inventive and coherent, though not quite at the level where every move feels startlingly inevitable in hindsight.</comment>
