Description remains purposeful rather than touristic, tightly braided with the story’s conceptual work.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Kael can discover the correct “equation” to meaningfully revive collapsing timelines—is clear, but the pressure curve is gentle. There is little sense of what happens if she fails today versus any other day, and no strong counterforce opposes her beyond abstract entropy. As a result, stakes stay conceptual rather than viscerally tense.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The thematic thread—hope as quantifiable, non-naive, and requiring ongoing recalibration against entropy—is unusually coherent, unfolding through concrete methods (gauges, balloons, shells) instead of pure abstraction. However, lines like “had proven that hope was neither naive nor abstract” verge on thesis-statement, slightly flattening subtext into statement. Even so, the piece leaves a strong conceptual afterimage about maintenance, not miracle, as the true work of hope.</comment>
