<question>3</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The grotto, tidal pool, rising tide, and star-mapped walls are vividly rendered and filtered through Mara’s sensibility as a tidal-pool chronicler, giving the setting both atmosphere and thematic weight. The jade cicada functions as a micro-setting object, a tactile node tying time, body, and memory together. While the environment doesn’t hard-constrain choices, it strongly charges the final image and tone.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—how Mara will position herself emotionally toward her brother’s impending departure—is clear, and the “pause” provides a tight temporal container. Escalation is subtle: the sustained silence, the rising tide, and her shifting insight gradually narrow her options from clinging or denial toward an earned acceptance. However, the pressure never bites very hard; there’s minimal sense that an alternate, more damaging response is truly live, so the felt cost is present but muted. The final exchange and his quieter voice do register as a meaningful, if low-key, tightening and release.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.9</grade><comment>Theme is strongly integrated: tidal pools, cicada, rising tide, and star charts all reinforce ideas of transition, cyclical time, and quiet, continuous change. Subtext is best where the marine imagery and the brother’s half-shadowed face do the work of showing exposure and vulnerability without commentary. The ending offers a resonant, non-literal answer—acceptance of transience as textured rather than catastrophic—though it flirts with over-articulation.</comment>
<question>6</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The third-person-limited voice is consistent and distinct: lyrical, image-driven, and firmly rooted in a mind that sees the world via marine and temporal metaphors. A few abstractions and repeated phrases (“texture of time,” “essence of transition periods”) slightly blunt momentum and edge toward didacticism. Still, there are no serious artifacts or loops, and the final lines land with a clean, charged image.</comment>

Some core insights about change and time are somewhat familiar in phrasing, but the specific constellation of setting and character gives them new texture. The story takes a stylistic risk with its dense lyricism and narrow temporal window, and largely makes that risk pay off.</comment>
