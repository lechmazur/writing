Environmental details evolve with events (ocean learning to breathe, lighthouse becoming monument) and clearly charge the final images. The world’s rules around silence and time feel consistent and learned through action rather than exposition.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The dominant stake—securing one final merciful moment at the end of time—is signaled early and remains coherent. Tension is more contemplative than visceral, so the escalation reads as deepening rather than sharply narrowing options. The cost is present but not sharply dramatized as a wrenching dilemma, which softens the felt intensity despite the cosmic scale.</comment>

Still, the piece mostly trusts image and situation to carry its conceptual weight.</comment>

Minor familiarity in some thematic language is outweighed by the freshness of the core imagery and method.</comment>
