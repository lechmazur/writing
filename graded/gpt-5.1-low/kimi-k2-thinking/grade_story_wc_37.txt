<question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The stakes—rekindling a dying realm versus sacrificing his remaining memory—are clear and scale appropriately for the piece. However, the pressure remains largely abstract and ceremonial; there’s little sense of risk or possible failure in the moment beyond the declared cost, and no concrete opposition or complication beyond “endless night.” The conflict reads more as an inevitable rite than as a contested struggle, which limits tension.</comment>
