<question>4</question><grade>6.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether the atrium (and time-cycle) freezes or renews—is clear, and the egg hatching plus fading starlight sketch a nominal clock. There is a single tightening (the vision of total ice versus lush future), but it doesn’t force a trade-off with felt cost, making the escalation mild. The story reads more as a graceful performance of a foregone duty than a test of commitment.</comment>
