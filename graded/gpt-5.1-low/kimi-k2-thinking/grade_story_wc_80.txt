The cost is conceptual and vocational rather than life-or-death, which fits the tone but keeps tension on the quieter side. A slightly sharper external pressure (e.g., an explicit institutional disincentive) could intensify the stakes.</comment>

That slight over-articulation prevents a higher score.</comment>
