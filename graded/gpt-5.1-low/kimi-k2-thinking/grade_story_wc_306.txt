The final decision to “wear [the cloak] more loosely” is a meaningful stance shift, but the cost of changing (or not changing) is only abstractly sketched. Overall, conflict is thoughtful but gentle, lacking a sharp test of commitment.</comment>

The symbolic system is clear but sometimes over-explained rather than allowed to resonate through image and action. The ending offers a satisfying conceptual answer, but it is told almost as a thesis rather than discovered obliquely.</comment>
