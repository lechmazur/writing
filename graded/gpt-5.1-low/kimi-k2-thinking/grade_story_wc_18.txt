The environment doesn’t much constrain tactical options, but it strongly charges mood and closure. Overall, atmosphere and specificity are a major strength.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—recovering and embodying a lost dream—is clear, and there is a discernible tightening as Kael shifts from searching to ritual making to unleashing the dream. The climax is beautiful but assured, so tension remains low-grade and primarily conceptual. Escalation thus feels graceful rather than gripping.</comment>

Still, the resonance is genuine and invites reflection without overt moralizing.</comment>
