The only limiter is that external physical constraints never corner the character as harshly as they might, so the environment’s pressure, while elegant, remains mostly atmospheric.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The central stake—preventing the caves’ maps and structures from becoming “useless echo” and finding a quiet that can hold both species—is clear and threads the piece. Because the beings cooperate quickly and no attempt visibly fails or exacts a sharp price, tension feels controlled rather than urgent. The resolution is fully protagonist-driven but comes with minimal sense of foreclosed alternatives or near-loss.</comment>

Recurring images—ice cracks, maps, bell-like arches—shift meaning by the end from pure threat to shared, managed weather. Complexity remains: storms continue, tending is ongoing, so the insight does not close into a pat moral.</comment>
