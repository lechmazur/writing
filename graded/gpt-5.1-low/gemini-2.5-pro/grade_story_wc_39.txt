<question>5</question><grade>8.3</grade><comment>Themes of attention, memory, and redeeming the unheard emerge organically from the particulars of jars, tide, and music box; the idea of “the small, daily sin of letting beauty die unheard” is potent. Still, the piece leaves a strong, contemplative aftertaste rooted in concrete images rather than pure abstraction.</comment>
