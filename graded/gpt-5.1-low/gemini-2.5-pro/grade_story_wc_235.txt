The flight sequence adds atmospheric altitude but functions more as interior metaphor than as a materially grounded landscape affecting tactics, keeping this just below the top tier.</comment>

There’s a distinct perceptual signature in how he notices time, memory, and physical detail, though a few abstractions (“my motivation had once been discovery”) slightly flatten an otherwise strong voice.</comment>
