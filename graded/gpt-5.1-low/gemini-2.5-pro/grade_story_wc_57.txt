<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—recovering and preserving an ancient method—is clear, and the knot’s three-day resistance gives a hint of prior pressure. However, there’s little sense of what is at risk if he fails today, and no strong reversal or narrowing of options beyond steady progress; tension stays low and cerebral. As a result, the conflict feels more meditative than pressured, limiting stakes intensity.</comment>
