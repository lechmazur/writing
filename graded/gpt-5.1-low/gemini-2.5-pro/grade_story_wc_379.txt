<question>4</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether the community will treat Mrs. Gable’s last correspondence with force or care—is clear and escalated by the threat of drilling. There is some complexity in the garden legacy and communal healing, but little moral friction or ambiguity.</comment>
