Specific locations in the city (bathroom mirror, bakery window, TV screen) further ground the abstraction. Setting is not mere backdrop; it shapes what’s possible in a way that feels organic and charged.</comment>

The city vignettes add emotional color but not real escalation or risk. As a result, tension is pleasant and lyrical rather than gripping.</comment>

The piece commits fully to its quiet, ritualized magic rather than undercutting it with meta-commentary.
