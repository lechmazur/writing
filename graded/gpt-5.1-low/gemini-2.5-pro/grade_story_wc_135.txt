<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether the past of this shattered city can be meaningfully reclaimed to sustain its survivors—is implicit but clear. The “turn” is more emotional recognition than conflict-driven escalation, so tension stays gentle rather than sharpening. <question>8</question><grade>7.9</grade><comment>The idea of an “ocean-spray archivist” reconstructing a ruined city’s history from tidal debris is an appealing, distinctive angle, and the touch-triggered visionary sense is a nice inflection.
