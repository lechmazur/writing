Still, the setting frames rather than constrains choices; it charges mood more than it pressures action, which holds the score below the very top band.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>5.5</grade><comment>The dominant stake—successfully transmitting a new, illicit identity outside surveilled channels—is clear, but it never meets on-page resistance.
