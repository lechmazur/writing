<question>3</question><grade>8.7</grade><comment>The mountain weather station is vividly rendered through selective, purposeful detail—the triple-glazed window, anemometer, satellite uplink, steel struts, frost, and aurora—filtered through Elara’s professional gaze. The weather and altitude subtly mirror the thematic arc of warming and glacial retreat without feeling like blunt allegory. <question>4</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Elara will continue to “occupy margins” and tolerate error or confront her complicity in climate collapse—is legible from early on and deepens as she links her fever, data anomalies, and the Edelweiss migration. Pressure escalates internally: from disorientation and doubt, to recognition that she has been smoothing away catastrophe, to a quiet but decisive stance shift in how she will record anomalies. The final act forecloses a prior path (rounding toward hope, hiding anomalies as tolerable fault) with emotional cost. The conflict remains largely interior and textural, so while strong, it never quite feels knife-edge urgent.</comment>

The closing image of auroral sorrow and a warming earth offers a resonant, non-literal answer to the story’s question about observation, denial, and responsibility. A few lines edge close to explicit thesis (“weight of complicity”), but overall the piece trusts images and situation to carry its argument.</comment>
<question>6</question><grade>8.6</grade><comment>The close third-person voice is distinct, lyrical, and coherent, with scientific lexicon (“isobars,” “logarithmic records,” “fault-tolerant oracle”) fused smoothly into heightened description. Psychic distance and temporal control are steady as the narration moves between present perception and fever retrospection. The elevated register is consistent and intentional, though its intensity leaves little room for contrast or roughness that might further humanize the voice.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The prose is polished and dense, with many sentences doing double or triple duty for character, setting, and theme; rhythm varies well and the final line lands cleanly. However, figurative language is nearly continuous, which risks saturation and occasionally edges toward over-richness where a plainer line might heighten impact by contrast. There are no obvious loops or scaffolding; the minor deduction is purely for density that could, in places, be trimmed without loss.</comment>

The conceptual move from technical “fault tolerance” to moral fault tolerance is especially sharp. It stops short of radical formal experimentation, but within a lyric single-scene frame this is fresh, memorable work.</comment>
