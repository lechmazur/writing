<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The central stake—resisting the void by capturing textures of time—is present early and tightens when the lamp’s hum “dips into a register that suggested the past was leaking out faster than he could gild it back into place.” That shift prompts his only clear escalation: he works faster while still maintaining restraint, racing to finish before the loss outpaces him.
