Internal and external conflict are braided but relatively gentle; she never faces a serious threat of failure beyond generalized difficulty and fizzled spells. The final image reweights the premise by framing success not as a miracle but as a painstakingly maintained rhythm, which deepens the story’s meditation on devotion and mediocrity. There’s rich resonance here without sermonizing, at a level suitable for high-tier venues.</comment>
