<question>4</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Mara can neutralize the curse and the fear parasitizing her guilt—is clear early and tightens as the faces of the dead appear and the luminance brightens. The main limitation is that the conflict path is relatively smooth once the rule is stated; there’s little on-page struggle or false step before she does exactly what the advisor prescribes.</comment>

Momentum is maintained across the piece, and the final image lands cleanly without being obscured by ornament.</comment>
