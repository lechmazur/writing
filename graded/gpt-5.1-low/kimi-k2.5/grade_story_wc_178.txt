<question>4</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The central pressure is Elior’s choice to move from safe observation and hoarding into active participation and risk in sealing the covenant. The stakes are high in a spiritual/epochal sense (new dynasty’s constellation, 26,000-year alignment), though they remain more metaphysical than viscerally tense.
