<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The primary conflict—how to respond to the broken kite—is gentle but real: she must choose between a false restoration and an honest, imperfect repair that honors her belief in brokenness. The cost—sacrificing her cherished feather, changing the kite’s behavior permanently—is meaningful but understated.</comment>
