<question>5</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>Themes of resistance to totalizing narratives, the politics of memory, and the commodification of bodies are organically woven through concrete particulars (the House of Twelve Sighs, Empire’s song, chastity as refusal, echoes as dissident memory).
