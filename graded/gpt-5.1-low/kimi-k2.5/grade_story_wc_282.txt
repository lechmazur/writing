The elm leaf fossil as micro-setting object nicely charges the final image and functions as an anchor across time. The world rules around “energy that defied instrumentation” are sketched but consistent enough for this lyric-SF mode.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>6.0</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Kael can meaningfully map and give voice to the world’s stored emotions for sleeping humanity—is present but remains abstract. There is little opposition or risk; Kael never seems in danger of failing or paying a price beyond effort, and the journey through the caves proceeds without genuine complication. The only real deepening is when the fossil warms and synchronizes with their heartbeat, which enriches the experience but doesn’t change options or force a trade-off. As a result, tension is more conceptual and atmospheric than felt as escalating pressure.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>Themes of memory, stewardship, and what it means to “witness” versus sleep through catastrophe are woven coherently through the fossil, caves, and mapping. Still, the final image of Kael rising with the fossil and golden convergence offers a resonant, non-literal answer about carrying forward the world’s felt history.</comment>

At times, the register leans slightly expository, but not to the point of breaking the voice.</comment>
<question>7</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>Line-level craft is generally strong: sentences are clear, varied, and carry multiple functions (worldbuilding, mood, and theme) with good information density. The prose occasionally stacks abstractions (“emotional topography of abandonment and hope”) where a more concrete beat could sharpen impact. Overall, rhythm and clarity hold, though a light tightening of repeated concepts would further elevate it.</comment>

The arc—an AI/being becoming “voice of the unknown” for absent humans—treads near familiar post-human witness tropes but is energized by the specific method (thermal spectra, song from maps) and cave setting.
