<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—escaping loneliness through genuine connection—is clear and intensifies from solitary work to fragile group, to fully committed community. Still, conflict remains mostly internal and atmospheric; obstacles are relatively soft, with little direct resistance or risk beyond implied emotional and environmental dangers. The result is strong thematic pressure but only moderate dramatic escalation.</comment>

The final assertion that art is meant “to outlast the loneliness” flirts with explicit moralizing but is anchored enough in the story’s particulars to feel earned rather than preachy. Subtext around territory, memory, and chosen family remains available for multiple readings.</comment>
