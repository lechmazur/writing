<question>4</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—testing whether absence can be proven to persist—is clear early and steadily pressured by the risk that nothing will answer back. The story avoids moralizing; instead it offers a charged conceptual answer to whether absence can be honored and known, with room for the world to remain indifferent. The emotional aftereffect is strong and particular, grounded in a meteorologist’s tools rather than generic grief tropes.</comment>
