Her realization about keeping only “the mask of unruliness, not the craft that keeps it true” beautifully ties personal arc to cultural failure. It’s complex and resonant without sermonizing, firmly in top-tier territory.</comment>

The only small limiter is that the voice holds one intense register almost throughout, with little modulation into plainer textures that might sharpen contrast.</comment>
