The final shift—admitting geometry never protected her and choosing to step into the arena with the stranger—reads as an earned, costly reorientation rather than a tidy epiphany. Closure lands cleanly as a Resonant Reconfiguration—she revises the function of her work from shield to experiment in mutual risk—and the cost (relinquishing self-protective logic) is legible. <question>3</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The optical illusion gallery is vivid, specific, and functional: bent corridors, suspended staircases, and vanishing corners all tangibly shape what visitors do and feel, rather than serving as décor. Environmental rules are consistent (rational enchantment, perspective-encoded instructions) and culminate in the central arena where geometry literally scripts acts of connection. The final image—the curve of two lines that meet without a sharp angle—derives directly from this rule-set and charges the closing choice. Sensory focus is mainly visual, but the spatial logic is so tightly integrated with character and theme that the environment feels indispensable.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Mara can transmute a logic-built refuge for loneliness into a site of genuine, mutual connection—emerges early and tightens as she threads the sinew and observes visitors’ small releases. The climax is quiet but real: she renounces the belief that geometry protects her and risks stepping onto the shared curve, foreclosing her prior stance of safe abstraction. The conflict remains mostly internal and gentle, but its escalation is clear and proportionate to the story’s scale.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.7</grade><comment>Theme is woven rather than stated: “rational enchantment,” math as “kinder than people,” and the rigging that encodes gestures into perspective tricks all embody the question of whether attention can be engineered into tenderness. The gallery’s past impacts (the grieving child, the forgiving violinist) complicate any simple take on illusion as mere escape, suggesting that constructed experiences can facilitate real emotional shifts. Mara’s final question—whether love might be “provable if tested with attention and risk”—rises organically from the concrete scenario rather than reading as grafted philosophy. The ending offers a resonant, non-literal answer in the curve they walk together, without collapsing into moral or slogan.</comment>

Lyricity is high but controlled; patterns like “not therapy, not theater, but…” show deliberate rhetorical shaping rather than drift. There is some risk of figurative saturation—nearly every line carries an image—which may slightly blunt impact in spots, but coherence and clarity are maintained. No significant scaffolding, loops, or grammatical slips appear, and the closure image is sharp and un-muddied.</comment>
