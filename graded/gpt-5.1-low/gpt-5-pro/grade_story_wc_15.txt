<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—finding a new path from communal ruin to reverence—is clear and present from early on, and the journey into the labyrinth deepens it meaningfully. However, opposition is gentle: the stone asks only for attention, and there’s little sense of what happens if she fails beyond a diffuse continuation of grief.
