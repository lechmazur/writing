<question>4</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>The central stake—whether Mara can understand and ethically mend the effect of her mistake—is signaled early and followed through. The cost is her exposure and relinquishment of the self-protective story that she only harms by accident. Because the conflict remains almost entirely internal and serenely staged, tension never quite tightens to a knife-edge, though it is coherent and meaningful.</comment>

The final image of walking to the patron’s door with “error cherished” offers a resonant, non-literal answer about how to hold sorrow and responsibility.</comment>
