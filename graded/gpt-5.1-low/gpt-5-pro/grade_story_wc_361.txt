The piece resists tidy moralizing by acknowledging hunger, dependency, and the seduction of stretched encounters even as it affirms restraint. The final lines reweight everything—errands as seeds, myth as utility, forgiveness as ongoing motion—without collapsing into abstraction.</comment>
