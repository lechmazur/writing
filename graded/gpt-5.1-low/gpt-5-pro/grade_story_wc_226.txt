Spatial and rule consistency are strong; the echo behavior becomes a world law that structures action. Stakes are morally and socially weighty, but the on-page price at the climax is mostly risk and fear rather than a visible loss or irreversible consequence. That slight softness of concrete cost at the end keeps the pressure just shy of top-tier.</comment>

However, lines like “Forgiveness, I called, is not a price you set and we pay” edge into direct thesis-statement territory, slightly flattening subtext at the peak. Even so, the closing repricing of forgiveness and grief is resonant and earns reflection.</comment>
