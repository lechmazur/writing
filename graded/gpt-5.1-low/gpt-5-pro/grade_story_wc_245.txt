Closure lands as a resonant reconfiguration: he now carries a reliable internal map and can guide others. World rules are consistent: vibration, tone, and pressure structure both navigation and revelation. The lushness is purposeful rather than touristic, though the atmosphere slightly outweighs immediate jeopardy.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—finding the stillpoint so he can survive emotional depths—is signaled early and revisited at the clearing. However, escalation remains mostly tonal and textural; there’s little sense that a genuinely harmful alternative outcome is near. The final gain is clear, but the on-page price and narrowing of options feel gentle rather than bracing.</comment>

The voice’s richness sustains pressure and clarity through the climax and reprise of the ring and blaze.</comment>
