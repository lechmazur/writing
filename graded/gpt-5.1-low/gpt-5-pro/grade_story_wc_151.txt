<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—getting Mira’s words safely past an invasive postal system—is clear, and the pressure coheres around that. The meaningful tightening is the realization that habitual margins betray senders, driving the choice to redesign both chalk code and envelope layout. The story pushes beyond tropey cyberpunk or generic dystopia toward something more intimate and idiosyncratic.
