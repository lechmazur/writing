The climax is beautiful and emotionally satisfying, but the tension curve remains relatively gentle.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>Theme emerges strongly through image and event: wonder as essential infrastructure, silence as a medium for courage and doubt, and art as a world-stabilizing act. Some lines verge on explicit thesis (“I had come here to certify wonder as essential”), but they’re supported by enough concrete, strange particulars that it doesn’t feel like sermonizing. The final tableau—with an unnamed face surfacing above and the hush curled beside her—offers a resonant, non-literal answer about how wonder enters and remains in a city.</comment>
