The closing return to road frost and the soft treatment of keys shows the setting’s logic persisting into daily life, charging the final image. Weather and materials (quartz, glass, ice, cooling metal) consistently shape both mood and method.</comment>

The conflict remains mostly internal and understated, so the tension is more subterranean hum than sharp spike, but it does deepen decisively. The price—reopening the “jar” of his father and accepting a non-rational apology—is legible and earned.</comment>
