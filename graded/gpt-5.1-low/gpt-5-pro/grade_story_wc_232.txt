World rules, including the fantastical doorway, are consistent and learned through action rather than exposition.</comment>

His decision to reopen the shop as a refuge and advisory hub is an earned resolution driven by his agency. The cost remains somewhat quiet and interior, with limited on-page friction between competing external obligations, which keeps this just shy of a higher band.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>8.5</grade><comment>Themes of agency versus drift, maps versus wandering, and loneliness versus communal responsibility emerge organically from concrete actions (following ants, reading maps of possible selves, opening the shop to neighbors). The final image of the doorway “not to escape but to enter choices” and the lantern-like promise is resonant, non-didactic, and emotionally honest. One line—“Loneliness had been a habit, not a fate, and his empirically intuitive mind named it an error”—edges toward explicit thesis, slightly flattening subtext.</comment>
