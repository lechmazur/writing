<question>4</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>The central stake—whether Elias can find a way to “understand” and thus manage letting go—emerges clearly and is tightened through the increasingly stark transmissions. A slightly stronger sense of what he loses or forecloses by abandoning the quest would deepen the stakes.</comment>

While the underlying idea—learning that not all things can be understood or controlled—is classic, the execution feels distinct and thoughtfully risk-taking.</comment>
