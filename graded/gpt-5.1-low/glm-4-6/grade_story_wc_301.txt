The recurring bellows-as-object also functions as a micro-setting anchor that charges the final image. Atmosphere consistently supports the contemplative yet raw tonal aim.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Elias can move toward self-forgiveness—emerges early and remains coherent. However, the “bad waters” never quite materialize as a concrete on-page test beyond spray and roar, so the external risk feels under-leveraged. The internal resolution is credible but a bit tidy, with limited sense of a price that will continue to bite after the scene.</comment>
