<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—liberating trapped creativity enough to gain rest—is legible and tracks throughout, and there is some escalation via the stars’ repeated urgings (“Again,” “Don’t stop”) and her mounting exhaustion. The notion of listening to unspoken star-words as a metaphor for latent, unheard creativity is strong. Sentence length and structure trend long and similar, which can dull momentum in what should be peak beats. Tightening repetition and pruning some metaphors would sharpen impact without sacrificing atmosphere.</comment>
