<question>5</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>Themes of wisdom vs. convenience, memory vs. technological amnesia, and collective rather than individual ascension are coherent and rooted in specific images (throne left empty, fruit with genetic memories, cities expanding into galaxies while wisdom lies hidden below).
