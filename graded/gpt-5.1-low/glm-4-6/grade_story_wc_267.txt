<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—learning what safety costs in this fortress—is present and sharpened by the revelation of vanished staff in the ledger. <question>5</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>Themes of institutional safety versus human cost, enforced silence as control, and erasure of stories emerge clearly from the piano, ledger, and vanished staff. However, the piece sometimes states its thesis directly (“the fortress’s safety came at the cost of its people, their stories silenced”) rather than fully trusting the imagery and situation to carry it. The resonance is strong but would be more literary-caliber if it leaned more on implication and less on explanation.</comment>
