<question>5</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>Theme is coherent—creative destruction as liberation from institutional control and a reinterpretation of vivification as enabling self-chosen life—but it is articulated very directly in exposition (“Creative destruction…is the only prayer worth answering”).
