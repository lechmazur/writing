Expository passages summarizing historical cosmology (Great Unraveling, creative axis) stay mostly within a reflective, archivist’s register, though they do at moments feel a bit report-like. Rhythm and syntax vary enough to avoid monotony and support the story’s quiet, accumulative mode.</comment>

There is occasional density in modifiers and abstract nouns, but it rarely clogs clarity or momentum within this reflective frame. I see no notable redundancy loops or scaffolding sentences; line-level craft meets high literary standards, just shy of the most exceptional polish.</comment>

The conceptual re-framing of the axis as metaphysical rather than physical, and the suggestion that art’s disappearance presaged dream extinction, is non-obvious yet coherent.
