Detail is selective rather than touristic, firmly filtered through the poet’s sensibility.</comment>

The key cost is conceptual/emotional (relinquishing the artwork to impermanence), which fits the lens but keeps pressure gentle. As a contemplative piece this works, though it lacks a truly tightening squeeze.</comment>

The ending leans into explicit statement rather than trusting the imagery to carry the weight, edging toward sentimentality. Thematic coherence is strong, but subtlety is limited.</comment>

However, the underlying emotional and thematic beats follow a well-trodden contemplative-epiphany pattern.
