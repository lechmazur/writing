<question>4</question><grade>6.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether he can resurrect and legitimize Isabella’s lost work—is clear, and the decoding-to-performance progression offers a basic escalation. As a result, tension remains modest and the story reads more as revelation than as conflict.</comment>
