The result is a lovely but low-tension progression rather than a strongly escalated one.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>Theme is coherent and organically threaded: restoration vs resurrection, solidity vs perception, certainty vs curiosity, all refracted through the nickel, the pause, and the garden. However, the story overstates its thesis in explicit lines (“Here, we trade what we know for what we can imagine,” “a reminder to trade certainty for wonder, always”), veering toward sermonizing instead of fully trusting subtext. That explicitness blunts what could otherwise be a subtler, more lingering resonance.</comment>

Some tropes (mysterious guide, return-from-vision wiser) are conventional, but they’re executed with enough specificity to avoid feeling generic.</comment>
