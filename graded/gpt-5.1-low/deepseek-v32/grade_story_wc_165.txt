Dialogue is brief yet natural enough, though it tends to mirror the thematic framing too directly. Overall, the voice is clear but not especially idiosyncratic or pressure-responsive.</comment>

Emotional stakes and character complexity remain modest, keeping the piece from feeling truly singular.
