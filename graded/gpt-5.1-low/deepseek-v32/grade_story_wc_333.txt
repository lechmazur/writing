<question>4</question><grade>7.3</grade><comment>The dominant stake—freeing an idea from fear—is established early and followed through. Still, the stakes remain highly abstract, and the locksmith’s internal risk calculus is underdeveloped, so the felt danger is more conceptual than visceral.
