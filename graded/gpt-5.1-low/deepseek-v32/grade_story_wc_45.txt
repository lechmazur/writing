<question>5</question><grade>5.8</grade><comment>There is a coherent thematic throughline about reframing loss, accepting mystery, and “turning mistakes into constellations,” and the tooth/constellation motif gives the ending some resonance. <question>7</question><grade>6.4</grade><comment>The prose is clean and grammatical, with some effective images (the curled body among roots, the tooth pulsing warm light, motes rising into a constellation). Still, much of the execution leans on familiar mythic-fantasy comforts: the kindly village healer, a benign ritual that works perfectly, offstage supernatural agents, and a tidy star-memorial ending.
