<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The central stake—preventing the town’s sonic and symbolic collapse—is clear from early on, and escalation is well handled via the intensifying hush and then the arch beginning to shed dust. The transformation of the “wild hush” from ominous to peaceful underscores a subtle thematic arc about silence as potential rather than void. That said, the piece largely affirms its central idea without much complication or counterpressure, which limits thematic ambiguity and depth.</comment>
