World-rules around time as fabric and silence as unraveling are consistent and learned through action rather than exposition dumps. <question>5</question><grade>8.4</grade><comment>Themes of sacrifice, memory, and art as a defiance of entropy emerge organically from Althea’s work with the Loom and her choice to weave her life into the message.
