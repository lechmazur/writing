<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Elm can find a truthful form for communal grief after a professional and emotional failure—is clear and resolved. However, the conflict is almost entirely internal and moves with relatively little resistance once the ghost appears; the story never truly threatens that Elm might refuse or misread the lesson. The piece avoids sentimentality overall, but a touch less direct explication would give the theme greater subtlety.</comment>
