However, Marin faces no serious trade-offs, and no live option is truly foreclosed on the page; she adjusts her model rather than making a costly choice. This reduces ambiguity and undercuts the chance for a more resonant, non-literal final reweighting. The insight feels gentle and comforting but somewhat platitudinous for a prestige context.</comment>

<question>8</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The central conceits—a “sea-foam recollectionist,” crater glass that transliterates emotions, migrating bird seeds that become memory-whispering flowers, and “cheerful gloom”—are striking and original. Where it falls short of the very highest band is in the familiarity of the final takeaway and the relative safety of the arc; the conceptual frame is fresher than the conclusion it supports.
