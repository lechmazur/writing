The environment actively charges the ritual and final reflection—the volcanic origin of the sand mirrors scars becoming new ground. A slight excess of similar-toned description keeps it just below the highest band, but the setting work is publication-ready.</comment>

This reads as a deepening reflection rather than a tightening conflict, so tension remains gentle and relatively static. The ending stance is coherent but not hard-won under pressure.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>Themes of healing, transformation of violence into care, and scars as music are coherent and carried consistently through images (black sand, lagoon, coin, scars). The emotional aftereffect is genuine but edges toward sermon-like repetition instead of trusting implication. A touch more restraint in the final third would heighten resonance significantly.</comment>

At times the register veers into essayistic abstraction that feels slightly more like authorial commentary than lived internal language. Overall, though, the POV and voice are polished and publication-viable.</comment>
