There are no orientation issues or causality breaks, but the stakes of the shift remain somewhat abstract.</comment>

However, the experience is uniformly positive and reassuring; there’s no serious test of boundaries or price, so tension never truly spikes. The ending’s “not a pill… but a method… a way… a willowglass hope” is resonant but somewhat declarative.</comment>

At times the voice leans toward essay-prose, but the perceptual signature remains coherent.</comment>
