<question>5</question><grade>7.6</grade><comment>Themes of knowledge-as-practice vs knowledge-as-text, and of absences as folded presences (“sealed awakenings”) are organically embedded in the plot and given a satisfying conceptual twist: noon shadows as folded light in a bent reality.
