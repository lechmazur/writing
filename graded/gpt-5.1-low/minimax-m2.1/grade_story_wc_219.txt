<question>5</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The core themes—homecoming as recognition rather than arrival, liminality between departure and destination, artificial versus genuine pilgrimage, and convergence across scales (insect, human, alien)—are coherent and nicely braided with recurrent images. However, the piece frequently states its ideas overtly (“Homecoming, Elena realized, was not arrival but recognition,” “The chemistry of homecoming was not merely biological but philosophical”), drifting into essayistic explanation rather than letting theme emerge purely from image and action. This explicitness blunts what could be a more haunting, oblique aftereffect.</comment>

Overall, the perceptual signature is clear and unified, even if modulation under peak beats could be slightly more varied.</comment>

Nonetheless, clarity is never in doubt, and the line-level craft is above-average and publication-ready with light tightening.</comment>
