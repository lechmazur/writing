However, the environment mostly decorates rather than constrains her choices; beyond the portal window, it rarely alters tactics or options. That keeps the setting from fully interlocking with the narrative stakes at a top-tier level.</comment>

The dawn deadline is mentioned but never truly bites—she succeeds without visible sacrifice or risk. As a result, conflict feels more ceremonial than dramatic.</comment>
