<question>5</question><grade>8.0</grade><comment>Theme around memory, historical erasure, and the ethics of witnessing is well integrated: Miriam’s civil-rights work parallels the grandmother’s temporal imprisonment, and the lagoon becomes a site where forgotten truths demand a custodian.
