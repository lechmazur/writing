The environment supports tone and theme more than it palpably constrains bodies and choices on the page.</comment>

<question>4</question><grade>7.3</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Maruk will reassert the covenant and thus shape the palace’s fate under the new regime—is clear, and the ticking clock of the dawn arrival provides a modest tightening. Still, external resistance is minimal and the ritual succeeds without friction, so the sense of risk or sacrifice never fully sharpens. The conflict remains more contemplative than pressured, limiting the felt stakes for a high-end short piece.</comment>

<question>5</question><grade>8.2</grade><comment>The thematic spine—frugality of meaning, the loss of devotion under purely empirical practice, and the quiet power of “encrypted” worship—is strong and organically tied to Maruk’s profession and the magic square. At times the narration edges toward explicit statement (“so focused on measurement alone, that they had failed to see…”), slightly reducing subtlety. Overall, the piece offers a coherent, resonant thematic answer without collapsing into moralizing.</comment>

Structurally, the story follows a fairly classical discovery → ritual → quiet aftermath pattern, so the innovation is more conceptual than formal. Overall, it offers a distinctive premise and thematic angle that could stand out, though it stops short of truly startling reinvention.</comment>
