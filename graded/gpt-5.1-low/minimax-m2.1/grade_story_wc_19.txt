The main tightening is her awareness of gravity returning, which nudges her into crystallizing her new stance, but the escalation from curiosity to resolution stays gentle. <question>5</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>Theme—about different kinds of silence, walls as protection vs. prison, and the reframing of “limitation” from scarcity to selective openness—emerges organically from Mira’s experiences and the repeating images (bunker, trellis, doll, sprout). However, the final paragraph edges toward explicit thesis (“not a bunker…but a trellis,” “the only thing limited was the fear of opening doors”), slightly reducing the lingering ambiguity that might elevate the piece further. The emotional aftertaste is real, but a bit more subtlety at the end would deepen it.</comment>
