<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether the caller moves toward or away from the site of her grief—is clearly signaled and sharpened by the north/south migration metaphor. Conflict, however, remains mostly conceptual; the journey itself contains no new obstacle, counter-force, or moral bind beyond the initial choice, so tension doesn’t escalate much past that moment of commitment. The cost of Mara’s stance is gestured at rather than tangibly tested in scene, keeping the stakes resonant but relatively quiet.</comment>
