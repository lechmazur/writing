<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—liberation and, implicitly, the release of a forbidden cure—is clear and integrated with her long-term strategy. The conflict functions more as a retrospective triumph than as a live, escalating contest, which keeps this from the highest band.</comment>
