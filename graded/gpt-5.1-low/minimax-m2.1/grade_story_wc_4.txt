<question>2</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>The story follows a single, coherent scene with an accumulative/textural drive: we move from arrival to the completion of work on one photograph and a reaffirmed mission. <question>4</question><grade>5.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether and how Maren can honor and “hold” the pain of forgotten workers—is clear but remains largely abstract and unopposed. The work on the torn photograph does not noticeably escalate in difficulty or cost; it’s a gentle deepening, not a tightening. As a result, pressure remains low and the piece operates more as a lyrical vignette than as a story with strong conflict.</comment>
