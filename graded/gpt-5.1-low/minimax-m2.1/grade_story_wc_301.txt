<question>2</question><grade>7.5</grade><comment>The structure is cleanly accumulative: travel to station → entry into bathroom → encounter with graffiti → reconfiguration of her understanding of her breakdown and persistence. Closure lands on the page with a clear before/after in how she interprets her “flooding” mind, though the journey to that realization is more told through reflection than dramatized through on-page choice under pressure. A slightly more concrete external hinge or micro-crisis would sharpen escalation and cost.</comment>

The abandoned infrastructure’s persistence (ivy not yet cracking walls, tracks still suggesting a path) subtly constrains and reflects her options and endurance. Setting is so integrated with theme and closure that removing this exact place would materially diminish the story’s logic and resonance.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.2</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether she can find a way to live with her mind post-breakdown without medication—is clear, and the discovery/reading of the graffiti is the primary tightening beat. The price she pays is accepting that flooding is part of her, not a moral failure, which forecloses the fantasy of total control or retroactive undoing. Still, the conflict remains relatively low-temperature; a more palpable present-tense risk or decision could heighten tension without breaking the lyric mode.</comment>
<question>5</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>Thematically, the piece coheres around breaking/continuing, non-apologetic flooding, and intergenerational mending, with strong image systems (river, ivy, accordion, patched bellows) carrying the weight. The final acceptance that she is allowed “to flood without apology and to flow without end” is resonant but also close to a spelled-out moral. With slightly more restraint in abstract commentary and more reliance on concrete implication, the thematic aftereffect could be exceptional.</comment>

Occasional phrases lean into therapy/essay register (“motivation had shifted,” “first fragile edges of self-forgiveness”), which slightly flattens the otherwise intimate, image-driven voice. Overall, POV control is confident and reliable, with effective modulation between external observation and interior reflection.</comment>

The closing paragraphs in particular balance texture and directness, landing a clear, memorable final cadence.</comment>
