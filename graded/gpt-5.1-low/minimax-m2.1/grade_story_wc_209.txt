<question>4</question><grade>6.5</grade><comment>The dominant stake is largely intellectual/spiritual—answering why flowers bloom at night—and the story honors that quieter mode of pressure. However, escalation is very gentle: there’s little sense of alternative outcomes or what she risks by failing to find an answer, so tension remains low-grade curiosity rather than genuine strain. The final choice to “begin to paint” is more affirmation than trade-off, with minimal on-page cost.</comment>

That said, the thematic statement is somewhat explicit and risks edging into moralizing rather than allowing subtext and images to carry all the weight. Still, the convergence of lunar cycles, encoded knowledge, and the closing garden image yields a resonant aftereffect.</comment>
