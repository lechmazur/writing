The setting doesn’t strongly constrain physical choices, but it decisively charges the final image when Maren presses the warmed columella to her cheek. World rules are consistent and legible within this gently speculative frame.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>6.8</grade><comment>The central stake—how to “capture” presence without killing it—is clear but remains almost entirely internal and philosophical, with no external counterforce. The whispered secret is framed as pivotal, but its content and stakes are elided, softening any sense of concrete consequence.</comment>

The final lines still leave a resonant afterimage of layered time and intimacy.</comment>
