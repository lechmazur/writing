The “tightening” consists mainly of climbing higher and noticing the scallop pattern in a new way, which leads smoothly to realization but without much sense of risk or genuine dilemma. <question>5</question><grade>7.0</grade><comment>Themes of forgiveness, duty, presence, and the non-finality of contentment are coherent and well-seeded in particulars (the garden, the hairpin, the ash tree, Aldric’s desperation). The final paragraphs, while emotionally satisfying, state the theme in abstract terms instead of trusting the images and actions to carry it.
