The recurring presence of the glass eye and the truffle mesh as physical objects charges the final images, especially the closing “glass eyes now” conceit. The glass eye briefly raises a moral conflict (“something that isn’t mine”) yet does not meaningfully alter tactics or options. Overall, tension steadily hums but never tightens into a moment where a significant option is clearly foreclosed with visible immediate cost.</comment>
