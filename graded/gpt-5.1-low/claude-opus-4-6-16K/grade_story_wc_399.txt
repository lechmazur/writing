A hair more interaction between bodies and environment under stress (e.g., consequences if the parapet faltered) would push it even higher.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.4</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether new prophecy will overwrite old wisdom—is well signaled and inherently strong. The conflict remains more conceptual than felt, limiting escalation despite the elegant premise.</comment>

The piece invites reflection on memory, progress, and stewardship without forced equivalence.</comment>

The final sense of shared occupancy on the “line” is a non-obvious, satisfying synthesis of the premise.
