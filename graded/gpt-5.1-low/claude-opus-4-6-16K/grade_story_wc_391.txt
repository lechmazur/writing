The price is legible: she sacrifices immediacy and cathartic confrontation for lonely, grinding labor. There is some explicit thematizing (“This was her patient revolution”) that edges toward statement, but it stays anchored in concrete circumstance. Complexity is preserved: the story endorses patience without erasing the validity and ongoing cost of her anger.</comment>

A few phrases approach the border of overt abstraction, but they do not obscure sense or dull the closure image.</comment>
