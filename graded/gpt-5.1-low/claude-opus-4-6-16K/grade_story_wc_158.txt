<question>3</question><grade>8.8</grade><comment>The gargantuan shell and its coral interior are vividly imagined and tightly fused with character and theme: the spiral, the bioluminescent “constellations,” and the throat imagery all matter to how we read Maren’s existence. This is deeply atmospheric without becoming a static backdrop.</comment>
<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—whether Maren can ever release her centuries-long vigilance and sleep—is clear, and the blossom armistice functions as a meaningful tightening of internal pressure. Her agency in writing the last poem and choosing to rest is evident, and the cost is the relinquishing of her long-held identity as insomniac witness. The piece could edge higher with one more concrete, immediate resistance or risk at the moment of that choice.</comment>

The emotional aftereffect is lingering, contemplative, and earned.</comment>
