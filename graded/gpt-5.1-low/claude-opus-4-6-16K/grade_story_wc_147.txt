<question>4</question><grade>7.8</grade><comment>The dominant stake—survival vs staying long enough to unlock and claim the temple’s secret—is clearly established and tightened by the oxygen clock (20 → 14 → 8 minutes). Internal and external pressures intertwine well; the temple’s onslaught of memories complicates her use of dwindling time. The eventual arrival of the rescue sub slightly undercuts pressure, because it resolves the external threat without being clearly earned by her on-page choices.</comment>

The explicit “Proof that the fleeting matters” line tips toward didactic explanation rather than leaving the image to carry the weight on its own. Even so, the piece largely trusts concrete images and situations to do the thematic work.</comment>
