Strong atmosphere, yet spatial choreography remains static.</comment>

However, complexity is muted—no counterforce questions whether wonder should be preserved, reducing thematic tension.</comment>

Elevated diction (“succor,” “verdant,” “ethereal”) fits her worldview, though near-purple prose risks opacity in peak beats (“radiant hush of the moment enveloped her”).</comment>
