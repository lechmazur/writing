Perceptual signature is strong: every observation is filtered through a sleepless maker who hears color and sees lullabies. Compression and elision (“centuries slipped by unnoticed”) are taught early and payoff in the closing cadence, sustaining legitimacy over clarity.</comment>

Closure line is clean, luminous, and propulsive.</comment>
